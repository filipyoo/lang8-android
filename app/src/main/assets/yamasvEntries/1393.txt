
Using らしいね

らしいね or そうらしいね is used when you've already heard the news the listener said.

天気予報によると大きな台風が近づいてるみたい。
らしいね。
- The weather report says that a big typhoon is coming.
- I've heard about that.

ケンが結婚するんだって。
らしいね。電話しておめでとうを言わなきゃ。
- Did you know that he's getting married?
- I knew. I'll call him to celebrate.
