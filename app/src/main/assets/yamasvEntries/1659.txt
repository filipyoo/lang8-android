
Using まま

まま can follow the ta-form of a verb to mean to keep/leave something as it is.

私は暗闇が怖いからいつも電気をつけたまま寝る。
- I'm afraid of the darkness, so I always sleep with the light on.

何時間も集中したままでいるのはむずかしい。
- It's difficult to keep concentration for hours.

彼は倒れたまままったく動かなかった。
- He fell down and never moved at all.

彼はエンジンを掛けたまま車を離れた。
- He left his car keeping the engine running.


まま is also used as an adverb that means "sometimes." It's often used with ある. 〜ことはままある means that something that is not supposed to happen sometimes happens.

写真と実物がまったく違うことはままある。
- It sometimes happens that something looks completely different in photos than it does.

ワールドカップでも弱いチームが強いチームに勝つことはままある。
- It sometimes happens that a weaker team wins a stronger team even in the World Cup.
