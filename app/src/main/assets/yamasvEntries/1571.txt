
Using 次の次

次の(つぎの) means "the next." You can say 次の次(つぎのつぎ) to mean the next but one.

次の次の駅で降りてください。
- You need to get off at the next but one station.

パリは次の次のオリンピックの候補地です。
- Paris is a candidate city for the next but one Olympic games.

Technically, you can say 次の次の次 to mean the next but two, but it sounds a little childish. You should say 3つ先.

Similarly, you can say 隣の隣.

先生の隣の隣に座っている女の子は私の娘です。
- The girl who is sitting next but one to the teacher is my daughter.

私の友達が隣の隣に引っ越してきます。
- One of my friends is moving to the house next but one to ours.
