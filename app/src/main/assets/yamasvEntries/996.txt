
Using 追われる

追われる is the passive form of 追う, which means "to chase/follow."

かわいそうなウサギがライオンに追われている。
- A poor rabbit is being chased by a lion.

彼は20年間警察に追われている。
- He has been wanted by police for 20 years.


It can be used with invisible things, such as 仕事, 時間, 宿題, 締め切り or so on.  It's used when you are very busy with something or feel pressure because of something.

私は仕事に追われていて、子どもたちと過ごす時間がない。
- I'm pressed by work and have no time to spend with my kids.

新聞社で働いていた時は、いつも締め切りに追われていた。
- I was always under a strict deadline when I worked for a newspaper office.


追われる is also used to mean "to be ousted."

彼はそのスキャンダルで社長の座を追われた。
- He was ousted as the president because of the scandal.

彼は軍事クーデターで政権を追われた前大統領です。
- He is the former president, who was deposed in a military coup.
