
Using 表に出る

表(おもて)に出る is a little formal expression to mean "to go out."

ここは危険な地域だから、夜は表に出ないほうがいい。
- This is a dangerous area. You should stay in the hotel at night.

It also means that "to appear in public."

彼はその国で最も重要な人物の１人だが、表に出たがらない。
- He's one of the most important people in that country, but he doesn't like to be in public.

彼が逮捕されたことはまだ表に出ていない。
- Nobody knows that he was arrested yet.

そのスキャンダルが表に出たら、彼は大統領を辞職せざるをえない。
- If the scandal is revealed in public, he has to resign the president.
