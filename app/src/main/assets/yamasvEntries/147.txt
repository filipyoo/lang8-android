
中でも / 中には

中でも is used to pick up something to stress from the group you said before. You can use it in both positive and negative sentences.

私は寿司が好きです。中でも、マグロが好きです。
Meaning: I like sushi, especially I like tuna.

私はスポーツをするのが好きではありません。中でも、球技は嫌いです。
Meaning: I don't like playing sports, especially I hate playing ball games.


中には means "in 〜", but 中には is also used to describe an exception. も is placed after the exception. You can use it in both positive and negative sentences.

日本人は寿司が好きですが、中には そうでない人 も います。
Meaning: Almost all Japanese people like sushi, but some of them don't.

日本人は英語を話すが苦手ですが、中には 得意な人 も います。
Meaning: Most Japanese people are not good at speak English well, but some of them can speak well.

選手の 中には、泣いている人 も います。
Meaning: Some players are crying. (Most players are not crying.)


You can't use 中には to describe what it's not rare.

東京にはたくさんの人がいます。中には、日本人もいます。
This sentence sounds weird, because everybody knows that a lot of Japanese people live in Tokyo.
