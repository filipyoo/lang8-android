
Words which start with 名(めい)

There are a lot of words which start with 名. Many of them means something great or famous:

名案 means a great idea. 
名優 means a great actor/actress. 
名場面 means a famous scene in a movie/novel. 
名人 means a master/expert. 
名所 means a famous sight place. 
名勝負 means great game/match. 

名(な) itself means "name," and it also means "fame." 名を残す means to leave your name in history, and 名を上げる means to gain fame.
