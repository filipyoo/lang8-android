
Using ～ないかな

～ないかな is used to express your wish or hope. Interestingly, although かな follows the negative form of a verb, it's used when you hope/wish the action happens. It's mainly used when you think out loud.

宝くじ、当たらないかな。
- I wish I won the lottery.

早くお父さん帰ってこないかな。
- I hope my dad comes home soon.

誰か僕にポルシェをくれないかな。
- I wish someone gave me a Porsche.

もっとまけてくれないかな。
- I hope they give me more discount.
