
Using 道理で（どうりで）

"道理で〜" means "No wonder 〜." It's used when you find out a reason for something. わけだ or はずだ are used with it.


彼のお父さんは、プロのサッカー選手だって知ってた？
道理で、スポーツが得意なわけだ。
- Did you know that his father is a professional soccer player?
- No wonder he is good at sports.

私は日本で生まれて、12才まで住んでました。
道理で、日本語が素晴らしいはずだ。
- I've been born in Japan and lived there since I was twelve.
- No wonder your Japanese is excellent.

私が泊まったホテルの部屋は、エアコンは壊れてるし風呂はカビが生えていた。道理で安かったわけだ。
- An air conditioner in my hotel room didn't work and bathroom was moldy. I understood why the room charge was cheap.
