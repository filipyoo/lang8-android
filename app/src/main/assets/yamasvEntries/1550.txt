
Using 糞

糞(くそ) means "poo". You can say "クソッ" to mean "Damm it!"or "Shit!," which is used when you make a mistake or something you don't hope happens.

It's also used to stress an adjective that has negative nuance. You shouldn't use it in formal situations because it sounds dirty.

今日は糞暑い。
- It's terribly hot today!

最近糞忙しい。
- I'm terribly busy these days.

この料理は糞不味い。
- This dish is nasty!

彼は真面目だ means that he's honest/genuine," but 彼は糞真面目だ means that he's too serious/square. It implies that he's inflexible.
