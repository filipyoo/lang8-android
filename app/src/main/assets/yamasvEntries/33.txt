
たい / ほしい(欲しい)

You can use たい when you want to do something.

私は日本に行き たい -  I want to go to Japan.
私はりんごが食べ たい - I want to eat apples.


You can also use たい when you want someone to do something. もらいたい or いただきたい is mainly used in this case. (いただきたい is a polite expression of もらいたい.)

料理を作ってもらい たい - I want you to make a dinner for me.
教えていただき たい I want you to tell me.


ほしい is used when you want someone to do something.

料理を作って ほしい - same as 料理を作ってもらいたい.
教えて ほしい -  same as 教えてもらいたい. 


You can use ほしい when you want to get something. がほしい follows a noun which you want to get.

新しいクルマが ほしい - I want a new car.
iPhoneが ほしい - I want an iPhone.


You can use ほしい when you express your wish. You can replace もらいたい instead of ほしい in this case.

その公園がきれいになって ほしい - I hope that park to become pretty and clean.
早く冬が終わって ほしい - I want winter to end soon.
