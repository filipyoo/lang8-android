
Using 寝かせる

寝かせる means to put someone to bed or have someone/something lie.

赤ちゃんを寝かせてからかけ直す。
- I'll call you back after putting my baby to bed.

医者は彼をソファに寝かせた。
- The doctor laid him on the couch.

It's also used to mean to let a dish or something like that stand to mature.

カレーを一晩寝かせるともっと美味しくなる。
- Letting curry sit overnight makes it better.

私たちはこの部屋でチーズを数ヶ月間寝かせます。
- We let cheese mature in this room for several months.
