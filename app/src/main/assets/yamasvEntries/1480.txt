
Using ちゃんと

ちゃんと is an adverb that means "carefully." It's a casual version of きちんと.

テストの時はちゃんと質問を読みなさい。
- You should read questions carefully on the test.

ちゃんと探したけど見つからなかった。
- I looked for it very carefully, but I didn't find it.


It also means "to be sure."

ちゃんとドアに鍵は掛けた？
- Are you sure that you locked the door?

ちゃんと彼に伝えました。
- I'm sure that I told it to him.


ちゃんとした means "formal" or "neat."

面接にはちゃんとした服を着て行きなさい。
- You had better wear neat clothes to a job interview.

お客さんと話すときはもっとちゃんとした言葉遣いをしたほうがいい。
- You should use more formal expression when you talk with your client.
