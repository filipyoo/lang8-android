
Using くらい To Mean "at least"

You can use くらい to mean "about." 

彼の娘は５才くらいです。
- His daughter is about five years old.


It's also used to mean "at least."

「すみません」くらい言うべきだ。
- You should at least say "sorry."

簡単な漢字くらい読めたらいいのに。
- I wish I could at least read easy kanji.

コーヒーくらい飲んでいったらどうですか？
- Why don't you at least have a cup of coffee before you go?


There is no major difference between くらい and ぐらい, so you can use ぐらい instead of くらい in all of the sentences above.
