
Using 〜たくなる

〜たくなる means to be tempted to do something. It follows the masu-stem of verbs.

漫画をたくさん読んでるうちに日本に行きたくなった。
- I was tempted to go to Japan while I was reading a lot of manga.

予告を見たら絶対あの映画を見たくなる。
- I bet you'll be tempted to watch that movie if you watch the trailer.

寿司を食べるといつも日本酒を飲みたくなる。
- I'm always tempted to drink Japanese sake when I eat sushi.

やるなと言われると、余計にやりたくなる。
- When you are told not to do something, you are more tempted to do it.
