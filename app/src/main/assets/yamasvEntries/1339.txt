
Using 伺う

伺う(うかがう) is a polite expression that means "to visit."

3時に事務所に伺います。
- I'll visit your office at three.

昨日伺った時は、彼は家にはいませんでした。
- He wasn't in his house when I visited there yesterday.


It's also used to mean to ask questions. It also sounds very polite.

2,3お伺いしたいことがあります。
- I have a few questions to ask you.

奥さんのことをお伺いしていいですか?
- May I ask you about your wife?

It's sounds slightly too polite to use it when you just ask something unimportant, such as how to get to the station.


〜と伺っています/伺いました means "I heard that 〜."

ゴルフが好きだと伺っています。
- I heard that you like golf.

娘さんが先月結婚されたと伺いました。
- I heard that your daughter got married last month.
