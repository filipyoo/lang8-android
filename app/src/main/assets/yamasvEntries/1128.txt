
Using かえって

かえって is used when what you did, trying to make something better, made it worse instead.

喉が渇いている時にソーダを飲むと、かえって喉が渇く。
- Drinking soda when you are thirsty makes you more thirsty.

節約するために自炊してみたけど、外食するよりかえってお金がかかった。
- I tried cooking my own meal to save money, but it actually took more money than eating out.

減量のために日本食をしばらく食べているけど、おいしいから食べ過ぎてかえって太ってしまった。
- I've eaten Japanese food for a while trying to lose weight, but I've actually gained extra weight because they are delicious and I always eat too much.

高速道路でゆっくり走るのはかえって危険だ。
- Driving slowly on a freeway is rather dangerous.
