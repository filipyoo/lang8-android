
Using 重ねる

重(かさ)ねる means to pile up or to stack.

あの塀は、石を重ねて作られている。
- That fence was made by piling up stones.


It's also often used when you repeat or continue some actions.

多くの失敗を重ねた後、ついに新しい機械を発明した。
- Finally, we have developed a new machine after a lot of failures. 

彼は貧しい家に生まれたが、苦労を重ねて医者になった。
- He was born in a poor family, but he became a doctor by overcoming a lot of difficulties.

議論を重ねているけど、まだ結論には達していない。
- Although we have repeated discussions, we haven't come to the conclusion yet.

警察は犯行を重ねている彼をまだ逮捕していない。
- The police hasn't arrested him, who repeats offenses, yet.


年を重ねる means to get older.

人は年を重ねると、賢くなる。
- The older you get, the wiser you become.
