
Using 大きい

大きい means "big;" it can also be used to describe a main reason of something.

日本語を勉強し始めたのは、英語に訳されてないマンガを読みたかったのが大きい。
- The main reason I started learning Japanese was that I wanted to read manga that wasn't translated into English.

アップルが世界で最も価値のある会社の1つになったのはiPhoneの成功が大きい。
- The success of the iPhone has made Apple one of the most valuable companies in the world.

田中が怪我してしばらく出れないのはヤンキースにとって大きい。
- The Yankees will have tough games because Tanaka was injured and can't play for a while.

最初の5分で1点取れたのは大きかった。
- It was a huge advantage for us that we scored a goal in the first five minutes.
