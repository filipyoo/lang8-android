
Using 勝手に

勝手に means "without permission."


勝手に私のノートPCを使わないで！
Don't use my laptop without asking me!

勝手に使っていいよ。
Feel free to use it.

彼は会社の重要な書類を勝手に持ちだしたので、解雇された。
He was fired because he took out the company's important documents without permission.


勝手に also means "automatically" or "by oneself."

この掃除機は、毎日決まった時間に勝手に部屋を掃除してくれます。
This vacuum cleaner automatically cleans your room at a set time every day.

タクシーのドアが勝手に開いたので驚いた。
I was surprised to see that the taxi door opened automatically.

今のはファールじゃない！　彼が勝手に転んだだけだ。
It wasn't a foul! He fell down by himself.
