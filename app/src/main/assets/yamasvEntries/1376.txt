
Using から to Mean "as little as"

から after a number can be used to mean "as little as."

レクサスは500万円から買えます。
- You can buy a Lexas as little as five million yen. 
This means that the price is five million yen, if you choose the lowest grade and don't add extra accessories or equipment. 

その試合のチケットの値段は500円からです。
- The price of a ticket of the match is as little as 500 yen. / The cheapest ticket of the match is 500 yen. 

注文は5箱からです。
- You need to order as little as five boxes.
