
Using たくさん

たくさん means "a lot of." You can't say たくさんな when it modifies nouns; you have to say たくさんの.

この工場ではたくさんの人が働いている。
A lot of people work in this factory.

やることがたくさんある。
I have a lot of things to do.


たくさん is also used to mean "enough." It's used with もう when you are disgusted.

漢字の勉強はもうたくさんだ。
- I don't want to learn kanji any more.

その話はもうたくさんだ。
- I've heard enough of it.

やめなさい！もうたくさんです。
Stop it. It's enough.

こんな嫌な経験はもうたくさんだ。
i don't want to have such a bad experience again.
