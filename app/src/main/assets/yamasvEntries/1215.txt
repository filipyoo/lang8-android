
Using ～という～ with a positive form.

(This post continues from the last one.)
When the same two nouns appear before and after という with a positive form. It stress "whole/all."

彼は私の財産という財産をすべて奪った。
- He stole all my properties.
(This is a stronger expression than "彼は私の財産をすべて奪った.")

彼は私の家の中の食べ物という食べ物をすべて一人で食べた。
- He ate all the foods in my house by himself.

私は地球上の山という山をすべて登ってみたい。
- I'd like to climb all the mountains on the earth.
- I'd like to climb every single mountain on the earth.
