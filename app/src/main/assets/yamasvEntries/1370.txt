
Using 当然

〜て当然 means "to deserve to do something."

毎日のように遅刻してるんだからクビになって当然だよ。
- You deserve to be fired for being late almost every day.

彼はノーベル賞をもらって当然だ。彼の研究は何百人もの命を救っている。
- He deserves to win the Novel prize; his research saves millions of lives.


It's also used when you have right to do something.

彼は怒って当然だ。
- He's right to be angry.

残業したんだから、残業手当をもらって当然だ。
- I worked overtime; I have right to receive the overtime pay.


当然の can modify a noun that follows it.

当然のことをしただけです。
- I just did the right thing.

残業代をもらうのは当然の権利だ。
- It's a natural right to receive the overtime pay.


当然 can be used to mean "reasonable/natural/of course."

宝くじに当たったら、当然ポルシェを買う。
- If I won the lottery, I'd buy a Porsche, of course. 

宝くじに当たったら、仕事を辞める？
当然。
- If you won the lottery, would you quit your job?
- Of course.

全然勉強してなかったんだから、テストに落ちて当然だ。
- You hadn't studied at all; it's quite natural that you couldn't pass the test.

彼は才能があるだけでなく他のどの選手より練習してきたから、金メダルをとったのも当然だ。
- No wonder he won the gold medal; not only is he gifted, but also had been practicing more than any other players.
