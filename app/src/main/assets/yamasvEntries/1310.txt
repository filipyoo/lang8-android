
Using お見通し

お見通し(おみとおし) means "to be able to see right through you." It's mainly used when you know something someone tries to hide from you. You can't omit the お even in a casual conversation.


お前のことは全てお見通しだ。
- I can see right through you.
-I know everything about you.

神様には全てお見通しだ。
- You can't hide anything from God.
