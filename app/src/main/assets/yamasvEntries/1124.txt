
Using 右に出る者はいない

右に出る者はいない is used when you are excellent in a specific field and no one can compare with you. にかけては follows a noun which indicates the field. I don't know why 右(right) is used in this expression.

チェスにかけては彼の右に出る者はいない。
- When it comes to chess, no one could beat him.

英語の文法にかけては彼の右に出る者はいない。
- No one can compare with him in the knowledge of English grammar.


You can use it for a company, country or something like this.

特許の数にかけてはこの会社の右に出る会社はない。
- No company has patents as much as this company.

顧客サービスの質にかけては、日本の右に出る国はない。
- When it comes to the quality of the customer service, Japan is absolutely the best.
