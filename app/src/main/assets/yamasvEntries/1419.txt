
Using 迎える

迎える(むかえる) means to welcome. 

来週留学生を迎える予定です。
- We are welcoming a foreign student next week.

ホストファミリーは私を暖かく迎えてくれた。
- My host family gave me a nice welcome.


It's also used when an event, mostly an important moment, is coming.

息子は5月に最初の誕生日を迎える。
- My son will reach his first birthday in May.

去年30回目の結婚記念日を迎えました。
- We had a 30th wedding anniversary last year.


迎えに行く means to pick someone up.

息子を学校に迎えに行く。
- I'll pick my son up at the school.

If someone picks you up, you need to say 迎えに来る.

明日迎えに来てくれる?
- Will you pick me up tomorrow?
