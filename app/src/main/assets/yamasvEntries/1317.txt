
Using 広い/狭い

広い means "wide," and 狭い means "narrow."

この通りはとても広い/狭い.
- This street is very wide/narrow.


Unlike in English, you can use them to talk about an area.

アメリカはすごく広いから自転車で横断するにはすごく時間がかかる。
- America is huge. It takes a long time to across by bike.

広い庭のある家に住みたい。
- I'd like to live in a house with a large garden.

僕の部屋は狭すぎてこのテーブルは置けない。
- My room is too small to hold this table.

この球場はほかの球場より狭いから、ホームランが出やすい。
- This baseball stadium is smaller than the others, so it's easier to hit a home run.

You can also say 大きい/小さい instead of 広い/狭い in these sentences, but it sounds a little childish.
