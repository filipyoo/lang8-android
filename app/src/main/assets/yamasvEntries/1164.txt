
Using 〜たでしょう?

"〜たでしょう?" is used when you know what the listener felt about an event even though you weren't there when the event happened. You need to say it with a rising intonation.

東京旅行は楽しかったでしょう?
- I know you enjoyed the trip to Tokyo.

そのニュースを聞いた時は驚いたでしょう?
- I know you were very surprised at the news.

その映画には感動したでしょう?
- I know you were impressed by the movie.

そのテストはあなたには簡単だったでしょう?
- I know the test was very easy for you.

お父さんが亡くなった時は大変だったでしょう?
- I know you had a hard time when your father passed away.
