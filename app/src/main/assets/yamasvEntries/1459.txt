
Using かたわら

かたわら is a noun that means "the side." It sounds formal and is mainly used in written Japanese.

本を読んでいる女の子のかたわらで猫が寝ている。
- A cat is sleeping at the side of a girl reading a book.

駅のかたわらに小さなカフェがある。
- There is a small cafe at the side of the station.

It's also used when you do two things at the same time. It follows the dictionary form of verbs in this case.

彼は銀行で働くかたわら小説を書いている。
- He writes novels while working for the bank.

彼女は子供を育てるかたわら大学に通っている。
- She goes to a university while raising a child.
