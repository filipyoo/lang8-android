
かねる / かねない


かねる in the following sentences mean "unable". Is is mainly used to express your intention.

あなたの意見には、賛成し かねる。
I disagree with your opinion.

その結果については責任を負い かねる。
I'm not responsible for the result.


かねない is a negative form of かねる. It is used to mean "might happen". It is mainly used in negative expressions.

こんな事がまた起こり かねない。
A thing like this could easily happen again.
(A thing in this sentence implies bad thing.)

彼は金もうけのためにはどんな事でもし かねない。
He would do anything for making money.
(Anything in this sentence implies bad thing.)

今にも雨が降り出し かねない。
It may rain at any moment.
(This sentence implies that you don't want to be rain. If you want to be rain, it is unnatural to say "降りだしかねない.")
