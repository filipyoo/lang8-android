
Using 癖がある

癖(くせ) means a habit. 癖がある means "to have the habit of 〜ing."

彼は爪を噛むくせがある。
- He has the habit of biting his nails.

彼は嘘をつく時目をそらす癖がある。
- He averts his eyes when he lies.


癖がある itself is also used when a character is too strong. It has a negative nuance. あの人は癖がある implies that they are quick tempered, difficult or something like that.

このお酒は癖があるから、嫌いな人もいる。
- This liquor has a strong flavor, so some people don't like it.

運転しやすい車を探しているならこのスポーツカーは癖があるからやめた方がいい。
- You shouldn't buy this sports car if you are looking for an easy driving one. This is difficult to drive.
