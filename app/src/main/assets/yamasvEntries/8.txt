
Add の/こと to An Adjective

I explained how convert a verb into a noun by adding の/こと yesterday.
I will explain what will happen if you add の/こと to an adjective.


あの大きい の が食べたい  -  I want to eat that big one.
大きい こと はいいことだ  -  It is good to be big.

赤い の は私のです  -  The red one is mine.
空が赤い こと  がある  -  The sky sometimes turns red.

おい, 若い の!  -  Hey, young man!
若い こと には利点がある  -  Youth has its advantage.

In the above instance, の following an adjective points an object which is qualified by the adjective. "An adjective + こと" expresses a state.


In this sentence, の expresses a state.
寒い の は平気だ  -  I don't mind the cold.
番号が赤い の は合格を意味する  -  Red number means you passed the exam.

In this sentence, の means a reason.
この本が汚い の は手あかのせいだ  -  This book is badly thumbed.
若い の は運動のおかげだ  -  An exercise makes me young.
