
空いている

空いている means "to be free / no plans." If you have no plans on a specific date/time, you can describe that "空いている." If you keep the date/time free, you can describe that "空けておく." If you had plans and you cancel it, you can describe that "空ける."

金曜の夜、空いてる? - Are you free Friday night?
うん - Yes.
歓迎会があるから空けておいて - Keep the night free because we are going to have a welcome party of new comers.


今日の午後、空いてる? - Are you free this afternoon?
いえ、打ち合わせがあります - No, I'll have a meeting.
クライアントが来るんだけど、空けられない? - Can't you cancel it? Our client will visit us.
分かりました。空けます - I see, I'll cancel it.  


開いている and 空いている have the same pronunciation "あいている." (開いている" is also pronounced as "ひらいている.")

全部の部屋のドアをちゃんと閉めた? Are you sure that you closed all rooms? 
会議室が開いてます - The door of the meeting room is open.

どこか部屋が空いてない？ - Is there any free room?
会議室が空いてます - The meeting room is free.
