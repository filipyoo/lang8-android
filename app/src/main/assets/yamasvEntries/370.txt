
The Meaning of 休む(やすむ)

休む has several meanings.

具合が悪いので今日は休みます。
I'll take the day off today because I feel sick.

"休み" is a noun meaning "holiday."

彼は今日は休みです - He is off today.
休みはいつからですか? - When will your vacation start?


休む is also used to mean "rest."

ずっと休んでないから少し休んだほうがいい。
You haven't rested for a long time; you should rest for a little while.

あのカフェでちょっと休もうよ。
Let's have a break at that cafe.


休む is also used to mean "sleep."

明日は早いから、もう休んだほうがいい。
You should go to bed because you'll have to wake up early tomorrow.
("朝早い" means "wake up early." You can omit "起きる.")

昨日はいつもより早く休んだ。
Yesterday, I went to bed earlier than usual.


休む also can be used to mean "stop." It's used in a negative form in this case.

地球は休むことなく太陽の周りを回っている。
The earth goes around the sun without stopping.

このコンピュータは24時間休まずに計算しています。
This computer calculates 24 hours a day.
