
〜たがる.

たがる is used to express that someone wants to do something.

彼はビールを飲みたがっている - He wants to drink beer.
弟は日本に行きたがっている - My brother is eager to go to Japan.


たがる is also used to express someone's habits or character.

母は甘いものばかり食べたがる - My mother always wants to eat sweets.
彼は自慢話をしたがる - He has a habit of boasting.


You can use たがる in negative expressions.

その子は人前で話したがらない - The kids don't like to speak in public.
弟が学校に行きたがらない - My brother doesn't want to go to school.


You can't use たがる when you are talking about yourself.

x 私はビールを飲みたがる
You need to say 私はビールが飲みたい.
