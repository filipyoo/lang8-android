
Using 落ち

落ち means the punchline. It's often written in kana.

そのジョークのオチが分からない。
- I don't get the punchline of the joke.


"〜のがオチ" is used to predict an unfortunate result.

チャンピオンに挑戦しても1ラウンドでKOされるのがオチだ。
- Even if you challenged the champion, he'd knock you out in one round.

彼女を誘っても、断られるのがオチだ。
- Even if I asked her out, she'd say no.

何を言っても嘘をついていると思われるのがオチだ。
- No matter what I said, they'd think I was lying.
