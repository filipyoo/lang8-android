
Using ごまんといる/ある

ごまんといる/ある is used to stress that there are a very large number of people or things. This ごまん has nothing to do with 五万, fifty thousand.

この国には日本車を持っている人がこまんといる。
- In this country, there are a large number of people who have a Japanese car.

トヨタと取引している会社はごまんとある。
- There are a lot of companies that do business with Toyota.

貧しい家に生まれながらも成功した人はごまんといる。
- There are a lot of people who were born in a poor family, but have succeeded.

彼はごまんといる候補者の中から選ばれた人です。
- He's the one who was chosen from a large number of candidates.
