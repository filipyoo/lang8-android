
ちなみに

We often use ちなみに in conversation. It is mainly used to add information to which you are talking about.

この本は、すごい面白かった。ちなみに、それはネットでも買えます。 - This book was very interesting. For your information, you can also find it online.

来月、日本に行きます。ちなみに、初めての海外旅行です。 - I will go to Japan next month. Incidentally, it will be my first trip to abroad.


You can use ちなみに when you ask a question which have relation to the speaker's topic.

A. この本はすごい面白かった - This book was very interesting. 
B. ちなみに、それはネットでも買えますか？ - By the way, can I buy it online?

A. 来月日本に行きます。- I will go to Japan next month. 
B. ちなみに、日本に行くのは何回目ですか？ By the way, how many times have you ever been to Japan?


Of course, it is very unnatural to use ちなみに before you begin to talk about something which has no connection with the previous topic.

x 来月、日本に行きます。ちなみに、今朝はコーヒーを飲みました。
x この本は、すごい面白かった。ちなみに、今朝はコーヒーを飲みました。
