
Using 手が空いている and 手一杯

"手が空いている" means that you have free time or you are not busy. On the other hand, "手一杯" means that you are busy or you have your hands full.

今、手が空いてる(あいている)?
- Do you have time?

今、手一杯です。
- I'm very busy now.

手が空いたら、手伝ってくれる？
- Could you help me when you get a chance?

日本語とフランス語の勉強で手一杯です。他の言語を勉強する暇はありません。
- I'm too busy studying Japanese and French. I have no time to study other languages.
