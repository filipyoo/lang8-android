
つもり / はず.

つもり is used to describe someone's plans or intentions.

来月、日本に行くつもりです。
I'm going to go to Japan next month.

明日は、早く起きるつもりです。
I'm going to get up early tomorrow.

彼は、その会議を正午に始めるつもりです。
He is going to start that conference at noon.

You cannot use it for things which cannot make decisions by itself.

x その会議は、正午に始まるつもりです。
That conference is supposed to start at noon.


On the other hand, はず is used when you have confidence. 

彼はその問題が解けるはずだ。
You must be able to solve the problem.

彼は２時間前に出たから、もう着いているはずです。
He must have arrived there, because he left two hours ago.

その会議は正午に始まるはずです。
That conference is supposed to start at noon.

You can use はず for yourself.

私にはできるはず。
I believe I can do it.
