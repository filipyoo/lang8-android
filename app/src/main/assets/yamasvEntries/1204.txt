
Using 体が空く

体が空(あ)く means you have nothing to do.

体が空いていたら手伝って。
- If you have nothing to do, please help me.

打ち合わせが中止になって体が空いたので、カフェで休憩した。
- I went to a cafe to have a break because the meeting was canceled and I had nothing to do.

やることがたくさんあって、来週まで体が空かない。
- I have a lot of things to do. I won't have free time until next week.
