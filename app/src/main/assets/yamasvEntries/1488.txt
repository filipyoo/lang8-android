
Using 見返り

見返り(みかえり) is a noun that means a return someone who do someone else a favor asks for.

彼は多くの貧しい人を助けたが、何も見返りは求めなかった。
- He helped a lot of poor people, but never asked for a return.

見返りに means "in return for."

市長は公共事業の発注の見返りに彼に賄賂を要求した。
- The mayor asked him for bribes in return for ordering a public work.


You can use お返し or お礼 when you do something in return for something else someone did for you. 

彼は公共事業の受注のお返しに市長に賄賂を渡した。
- He gave the mayor bribes in return for receiving a public work.

日本語を教えてくれたらお返しに英語を教えます。
- If you teach me Japanese, I'll teach you English in return.

一晩泊めてもらったお礼に彼の部屋を掃除した。
- I cleaned his room in return for letting me stay for one night.
