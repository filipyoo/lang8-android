
How to Say "For The First Time" in Japanese.

1. 〜ぶり means "for the first time in 〜."

10年ぶりに日本に行く.
I'm going to Japan for the first time in a decade.

6ヶ月ぶりに彼に会う.
I'll meet him for the first time in 6 months.


2.  〜以来 means "for the first time since 〜."

子供の時以来初めて日本に行く.
(日本に行くのは、子供の時以来です.)
I'm going to Japan for the first time since I was a child

卒業以来初めて彼に会う.
(彼に会うのは、卒業以来です.)
I'll meet him for the first time since graduation.


3. 生まれて初めて means "for the first time in one's life."

生まれて初めて日本に行く.
I'm going to Japan for the first time in my life.

生まれて初めてに彼に会う.
I'll meet him for the first time in my life.


4. "{今世紀 | 今年 | 今月 ...} になってはじめて" means "for the first time (in) this {century | year | month ....}

今世紀 になって初めて、南半球でオリンピックが開かれる。
The Olympic games will be held in the Southern Hemisphere for the first time in this century.

今年 になって初めて、今日雪が降った。
It snowed today for the first time this year.
