
Using グル

グル means "a conspirator." It has a negative nuance, and is usually written in katakana.

彼は私の妻とグルになって私を騙した。
- He deceived me in cahoots with my wife.

誰も信用するな。彼らはみんなグルだ。
- Don't trust any of them. They're all in it together.

その詐欺師の10才の娘までグルだった。
- Even the swindler's 10-year-old daughter was also his conspirator.
