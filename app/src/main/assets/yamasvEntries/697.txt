
Using と言うのも

と言うのも is placed at the top of a sentence, and is used to describe a reason of the sentence that is placed before. A redundant version of this would be "なぜ私がそう言うかというと."


彼は犯人じゃない。と言うのも、何人かの人が殺人現場から離れたバーでその時間に彼を見ているからだ。
- He cannot be the criminal because some people saw him at a bar that is far from the place of the murder at the time. 

私は4月に日本に行きたい。と言うのも、友達が日本の桜はものすごくきれいだって言ってたから。
- I'd like to visit Japan in April because my friend told me that cherry blossoms in there were really beautiful.

その国への旅行は延期した方がいい。と言うのも、TVのニュースでそこで伝染病が発生したって言ってたから。
- You should postpone the trip to the country because TV news said that an epidemic disease broke out there.


You can also say と言うのは instead.
