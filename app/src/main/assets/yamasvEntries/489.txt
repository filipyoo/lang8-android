
〜なんてもんじゃない

"〜なんてもんじゃない" is a casual expression to mean "〜 is an understatement." It can follow a noun, a verb with an adverb, and an adjective.

優秀なんてもんじゃない。彼は本当の天才だ。
Excellence is an understatement. He's a real genius.

彼女は日本語を少し知ってるなんてもんじゃない。ネイティブのように完璧に話せる。
She doesn't "know a little Japanese." She can speak perfectly like a native speaker.

彼の家は大きいなんてもんじゃない。まるで城のようだ。
"Big" isn't enough to describe how big his house is. That looks like a castle.
