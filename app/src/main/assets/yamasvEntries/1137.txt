
Using とっておきの

とっておきの is used to modify something special. It implies that you don't want to introduce it to anyone but someone important to you.

東京に来た時にはとっておきの寿司屋に連れて行くよ。
- I'll take you the best sushi restaurant in the world when you visit Tokyo.

彼との最初のデートにとっておきのドレスを着ていったのに、彼は安いラーメン屋に連れて行った。
- Although I wore a special dress on the first date with him, he took me a cheap ramen restaurant. 

とっておきのジョークを聞きたい？ 絶対笑いが止まらないよ。
- Do you want to hear the best joke? I bet you wouldn't stop laughing.

息子の21才の誕生日にとっておきのワインをプレゼントするつもりだ。
- On my son's 21st birthday, I'm going to present him a wine I kept for a special occasion.
