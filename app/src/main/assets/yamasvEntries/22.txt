
覚えている / 思い出す

You can translate "remember" into 覚えている and 思い出す.

思い出す means to bring back to your mind information that you knew.
覚えている means to keep information in your mind.

Examples:
君のことは思い出せない - I don't recollect you.
彼の名が思い出せない - His name escapes me.
今でも彼を覚えている - I still remember him even now.

I think you can translate "I remember him" into two separate sentences:
"彼を覚えている" and "彼を思い出す."
I think it is impossible to translate exactly without a context. Am I right?


覚える means to learn/memorize.
漢字を覚える - I learn kanji.
単語を覚えるのが好きだ - I like to memorize new vocabulary.
