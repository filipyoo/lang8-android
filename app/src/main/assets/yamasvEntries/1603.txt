
Using ～として

You can say ～として to describe assumption. It follows a phrase.

東京に滞在するのに1日100ドルかかるとして、1ヶ月滞在するには3000ドル用意しないといけない。
- Suppose it costs one hundred dollars a day to stay in Tokyo. We have to prepare three thousand dollars to stay there one month.

彼と私が付き合っていたとして、それの何が問題なの？
- Let's say he and I were seeing. What was the problem with that?

私が億万長者だとして、有効に使うためには何をすればいいんだろう？
- Let's say I were a billionaire. What should I have done to use the money effectively.

ポルシェを買う金があるとして、そんな高い車を維持できるの？
- Even if you have enough money to buy a Porsche, do you think you could maintain that expensive car? 


仮に is often used with として; you can add 仮に at the top of all the sentences above, but it's not necessary because として implies 仮に.
