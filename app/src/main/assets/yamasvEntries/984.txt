
Using 二の次

二の次 (にのつぎ) is used when you think that something isn't so much important.

なんで彼女はあのブサイクな男と付き合ってるの？
金持ちだから。彼女にとって見た目は二の次。
- Why she is seeing the ugly guy?
- It's because he is rich. It's not important for her how he looks.


安いノートPCを探している。性能は二の次だ。
- I'm looking for a cheap laptop computer. The performance isn't the best important thing.


どうしてもソフトウェアエンジニアとして働きたい。給料は二の次だ。
- I really want to work as a software engineer. The salary is secondary.
