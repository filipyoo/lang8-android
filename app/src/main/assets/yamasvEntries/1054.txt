
Using 以下, 以上, 以内 and 以外


以下 means "not more than." It includes the number itself before it.

５以下の正の自然数は1,2,3,4,5です。
- The positive natural numbers not more than 5 are 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5.

未満 means "less than." It doesn't include the number before it.

５未満の正の自然数は1,2,3,4です。
- The positive natural numbers less than 5 are 1, 2, 3, and 4.


以上 means "not less than." It includes the number itself before it.

３以上５以下の正の自然数は3,4,5です。
- The positive natural numbers not less than 3 and not more than 5 are 3, 4 and 5.


以内 means "within." It follows a period of time or a distance.

それは５日以内に終わる。
- It'll finish within five days.

駅から100メートル以内のところに住んでいます。
- I live within 100 meters of the station.


The opposite of 以内 is 以上.

それは５日以上かかる。
- It'll take at least 5 days.

駅から2キロ以上のところに住んでいます。
- I live over 2 kilometers away from the station.


以外 means "except." Unlike the rest words, it can follow a noun.

私以外のメンバーは日本語が話せる。
- All members except me speak Japanese.

彼以外は誰もそれを知らない。
- Nobody but him knows it.

私の部屋にはベッド以外は何もない。
- There is nothing but a bed in my room.
