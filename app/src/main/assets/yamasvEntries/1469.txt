
Using 卒業する

卒業する means to graduate.

来月大学を卒業します。
- I'm going to graduate from university next month.

It's used as a metaphor meaning to stop doing something and go to the next step.

うちの娘はもうおむつは卒業した。
- My daughter has already stopped wearing diapers.

マンガは卒業しなさい。もう30なんだから。
- You should stop reading manga. You're already 30 years old.

１日も早く独身を卒業したい。
- I'd like to get married as soon as possible.
(独身 means a single.)

ゲームはもう卒業した。
- I was into video games, but I don't play them any more.

簡単な挨拶は卒業して、もっと複雑なフレーズが言えるようになりたい。
- I can't speak Japanese except simple greetings. I'd like to master more complicated phrases.
