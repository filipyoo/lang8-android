
Using 〜がり屋

目立ちたがり屋 means a person who likes to be the center of attention. 〜がり屋 is used to describe a person's characteristics. 

彼女は寂しがり屋だ。
- She always needs company. / She can't stand begin alone.

彼は恥ずかしがり屋だ。
- He is shy.

私は寒がり/暑がり屋だ。
- I'm a person who is sensitive the cold/heat.

彼は面倒くさがり屋だ。
- He is lazy.
