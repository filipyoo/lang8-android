
Using 心地(ここち)

心地 means "a feeling." 

生きた心地がしなかった。
- I felt as if I was dead.


心地よい means "to be comfortable."

風が心地よかったから、バスに乗らずに歩いて家に帰った。
- I walked home instead of taking the bus because the wind was comfortable.


心地 can follow the masu stem of verbs. It's pronounced as "ごこち" in this case.

このベンチは座り心地(すわりごこち)が悪い。
- This bench is uncomfortable. / I don't feel comfortable when sitting on this bench."

このベッドは寝心地(ねごこち)がいい。
- This bed is comfortable. / I feel comfortable when sleeping on this bed.
