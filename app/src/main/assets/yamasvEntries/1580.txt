
Using まちまち

まちまち is used when somethings are not the same.

役員のその問題に対する意見はまちまちだ。
- Each board member has different opinion about the issue.

彼はいつも朝７時に会社に行くが、帰ってくる時間はまちまちだ。
- He goes office at seven every morning, but doesn't come home at a specific time.

そのTV番組の視聴者はほとんど女性ですが、年齢はまちまちです。
- Most viewers of the TV show are women, but the range of their ages is wide.

ラーメンのスープの作り方は店によってまちまちです。
- Every ramen restaurant has their own way to make the soup.
