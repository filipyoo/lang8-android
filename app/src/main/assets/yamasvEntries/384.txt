
The Meaning of そのうち

そのうち means "soon." It may be within a few minutes, it may also be within a few decades. That depends on the context.

そのうち彼は来るよ.
He'll come soon.

そのうち会いましょう.
See you later.

そのうち彼はノーベル賞をとるよ.
He'll win the Novel Prize in the near future.

毎日練習すれば、そのうち流暢に日本語を話せるようになるよ.
You will be able to speak Japanese fluentry before long if you practice every day.

そのうち月に住めるようになるかもしれない.
It may be possible that we can live in the moon in the future.
