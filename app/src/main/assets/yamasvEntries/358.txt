
Common Mistakes About 始めます

学校を1月に始めます。
I'll start my school in January.

夏休みを昨日から始めました。
I started my summer vacation yesterday.

The above two Japanese sentences sound unnatural. You should say:
学校は1月に始まります,
夏休みは昨日から始まりました。

毎日仕事を9時に始めます - I start my job at nine o'clock every day.
You can say that If you are a freelancer or something and you can control your working time. However, you should say "仕事は毎日9時に始まります" if you can't control your working time.
