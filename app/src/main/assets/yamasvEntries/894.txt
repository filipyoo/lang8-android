
Using 〜だらけ

〜だらけ means to be full of something.

日本は自動販売機とコンビニだらけです。
- Japan is full of vending machines and convenience stores.

この教科書は間違いだらけだ。お金を返して欲しい。
- There are a lot of mistakes in this textbook. I want them to pay back my money.

最近この街は観光客だらけだ。
- Lately, this town is full of tourists.

この会社は借金だらけだ。そのうち倒産するだろう。
- This company has a lot of debts. It will go bankrupt in the near future.


You can also use it when you are covered with something.

道に倒れている男は血だらけだった。
The man lying on the road was covered with blood.

子供達が泥だらけになって帰ってきた。
- Our kids came home covered in mud.
