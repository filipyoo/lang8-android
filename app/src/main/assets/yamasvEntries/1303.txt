
Using ～構わず

構わず means "not care." 
所構わず(ところかまわず) means "anywhere/everywhere."

彼は所構わずタバコを捨てる。
- No matter where he is, he throws away his cigarette bull.

壁に所構わず落書きがしてある。
- You can see a lot of graffiti all over the wall.


辺り構わず(あたりかまわず) means "without concern for what people might think."

彼女は電車の中で辺り構わず突然大声で歌い出した。
- She suddenly started singing loudly even though there were a lot of people in the train.

彼は辺り構わずおならをする。
- He farts in front of people without hesitation.


誰彼構わず(だれかれかまわず) means "to anybody/everybody."

彼は酔っ払うと誰彼構わず女性に声をかける。
- When he's drunk, he talks to every girl.

彼女は秘密を守ることができない。誰彼構わずしゃべってしまう。
- She can't keep any secret. She tells everybody. 


なりふり構わず means "don't care how you look."

彼はサッカーのプロになるために、なりふり構わず練習している。
- He's practicing soccer very hard to become a professional player. He doesn't care how he looks at all.

彼女は息子を大学に行かせるためになりふり構わず働いている。
- She has been working like a dog to send her son to a college.


所構わず, 辺り構わず and 誰彼構わず have a negative nuance. なりふり構わず itself also has a negative one, but it's often used in a positive sentence.
