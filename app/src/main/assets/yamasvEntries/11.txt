
Postpositional Particles after Date/Time

1:
The next sentences all describe "This shop is closed at noon."

正午　まで　　　営業する
正午　までで　　閉店する
正午　に　　　　閉店する
正午　までが　　営業時間だ
正午　が　　　　閉店時間だ
正午　で　　　　閉店する
正午　でもって　閉店する
正午　までを　　営業時間とする

2:
The next sentences describe "This shop is closed  by noon.   (It may be closed at 11:00am.)"

正午　までに　閉店する    
正午　までには　閉店する    

3:
The next sentence describes "This shop is open during the morning. (The closing time is ambiguous.)"

正午　までは　　営業する

If you say "正午まではここにいる", We feel that you will stay there in the morning and leave there at noon, but you may stay there for a short time after noon.



Additional information about before/after noon:

午前に電話する - I will call you in the morning. 
正午に電話する - I will call you at noon.
午後に電話する - I will call you in the afternoon.

If you say 午前に, we feel it will be about 9:00am - 12:00am.
If you say 正午前に, we feel it will be about 11:30am - 12:00am.
We don't say 正午後.
