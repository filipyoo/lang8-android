
Using こと and もの

こと can be used to refer to an action; もの can be used to refer to a thing.

この国ではできないおもしろいことを日本でしたい。
I'd like to do something interesting in Japan I can't do in my country.

この国では食べられないおもしろいものを日本で食べたい。
I'd like to eat something interesting in Japan I can't eat in my country.

今日変わったことがあった。
I had an unusual experience today.

今日変わったものを見た。
I saw an unusual thing today.

日本で一番おもしろかったことは何ですか？
What was the most interesting thing you did in Japan?

日本で一番おもしろかったものは何ですか？
What was the most interesting thing you saw in Japan?
