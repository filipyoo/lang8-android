
Using 今思えば

今思えば(いまおもえば) is used when you realize that what you was thinking was wrong. You can also say 今思うと or 今にしてみれば instead.

今思えば、彼はいいやつだった。
- Now if I think about it, he was a good guy.
This sentence implies that you didn't think that he was a good guy before.

今思えば、僕は馬鹿だった。
- Looking back now, I was stupid.

今思えば、あんなに働く必要はなかった。もっと家族と過ごすべきだった。
- Thinking back on it, I didn't have to work that hard. I should've had more time with my family.
