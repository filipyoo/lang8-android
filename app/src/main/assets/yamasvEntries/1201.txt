
Using 〜の結果

その精密検査は私が癌だと明らかにした。
- The detailed examination revealed that I have a cancer.

This translation is correct, but sounds a little unnatural. 
"精密検査の結果、私は癌だとわかった" sounds more natural.

捜査の結果、役員の殆どが不正会計に関わっていたとわかった。
- The investigation revealed that most of the board members were involved in the improper bookkeeping.

20年に渡る研究の結果、火星に微生物がいることがわかった。
- The research, which has been ongoing for 20 years, revealed that some microorganisms live on the Mars.
