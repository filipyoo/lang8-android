
Using 荷が重い

The literal meaning of 荷が重い is "the luggage is heavy." It's used when you feel a pressure or are not confident to play a role.

この部署の管理は私には荷が重い。
- I'm not confident to manage this section.

役員の前で日本語でプレゼンするのは荷が重い。
- I'm under the pressure. I have to make a presentation in Japanese in front of the board members.

私には監督は荷が重い。
- You are asking for too much. I'm not the right person to become the head coach.

彼に諦めるように説得するのは荷が重い。
- I feel that it’s a big burden to persuade him to give it up.
