
Using 仕切る

仕切る means to partition, separate or divide.

その部屋はカーテンで２つに仕切られている。
- The room is separated into by the curtain.

２つの空間を仕切るものは何もない。
- There is no partition between the two spaces.


It's also used to mean to manage or handle something.

新聞によると、社長がその不正会計を仕切っていたらしい。
- The newspaper says that the improper bookkeeping was managed by the president.

禁酒法時代はマフィアが酒の密輸を仕切っていた。
- During the prohibition era, the mafia managed to smuggle liquor.
 
社長が入院しているので、今は副社長が会社を仕切っている。
- Since the president is in the hospital, the vice president manages the company now.

その交渉は彼が独りで仕切っている。
- He's handling the negotiation by himself.

仕切る has a slightly negative nuance. 彼は仕切り屋だ means "He likes to control everything." It implies that the speaker doesn't like the character.
