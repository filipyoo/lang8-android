
Using 今に

今に means "in the near future." It's mainly used when you describe your prediction.

彼は今に世界記録を更新する。
- I'm sure that he'll establish a new world record in the near future.

彼は今にクビになるだろう。
- He'll be fired before long.

私が正しいことは今にわかる。
- You'll know that I'm right soon.


今にも is used when something looks as if it is about to do something.

あの木は今にも倒れそうだ。
- That tree looks as if it's about to fall.

あの船は今にも沈みそうだ。
- That boat is on the point of sinking.
