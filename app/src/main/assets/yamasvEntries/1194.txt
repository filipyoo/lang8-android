
Using 今や

今や is used to stress "now." It implies something has dramatically changed.

20年前に大学の寮で2人の学生が立ち上げたグーグルは、今や世界で最も価値のある会社の1つだ。
- Google, started by two students in a dorm twenty years ago, is one of the most valuable companies in the world now.

彼は最も有名なフットボール選手だったが、今や殺人犯として知られている。
- He was the most famous football player, but, now, he's known as a murderer.

彼は社長として月に800万円稼いでいたが、逮捕されて今や刑務所ぐらしだ。
- Although he earned eight million yen a month as the president, he was arrested and is in a jail now.
