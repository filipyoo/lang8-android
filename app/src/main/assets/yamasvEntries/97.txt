
向き(むき) / 向け（むけ)

向き means "suit for."

やさしい日本語で書かれているので、この本は初心者 向き です。
Written in simple Japanese, this book is suitable for beginners.

このシャツは派手すぎるので、若い人 向き です。
Because this shirt is too loud, it is suitable for young people.

彼は話し好きなので、セールスマン 向き だと思う。
I think he is suited to be a salesman, because he likes to talk.

このワインは、魚料理とよく合う(このワインは、魚料理 向き だ)。
This wine goes well with fish dishes.



向け means "made for." It is used when a producer create something for specific target consciously. 

これは女性 向け の車です。
This car is designed for women.

この製品は Mac 向け です。WindowsのPCでは使えません。
This product is for Mac only. You can't use it with Windows computers.

作者は、初心者 向け に、このテキストを書いた。
The writer wrote this textbook for beginners.
（This sentence implies the writer wrote it for only beginners. If you say "この本は初心者'向き'だ," it implies you don't know the writer's intention.)
