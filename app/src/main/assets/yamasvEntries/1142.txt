
Using くれぐれも

くれぐれも is used to make sure of something. It's often used with a negative form.

くれぐれもパスポートを忘れないように。
- Make sure not to forget your passport.

くれぐれも飲み過ぎないように。
- Make sure not to drink too much.

あの地域は危険です。くれぐれも夜に１人で行かないでください。
- That area is dangerous. Make sure not to go there alone at night.

奥さんにくれぐれもよろしく。
- Make sure to say hello to your wife.
