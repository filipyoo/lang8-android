
The Usage of 例えばの話

例えば is used to list examples.

最近では、ヨーロッパでプレーしている日本人のサッカー選手はたくさんいます。例えば、香川や本田。
- In these days, there are a lot of Japanese football players who play in Europe; for example, Kagawa and Honda.


"例えばの話" is used as a preamble when you talk about hypothetical situations.

例えばの話、突然首になったらどうしますか？
I have a hypothetical question for you. What would you do if you were suddenly fired?

例えばの話、グーグルがすべてのサービスを停止したら、多くの人がパニックになるだろう。
If Google stopped all services, a lot of people would go into a panic.

例えばの話、アメリカの好きなところに住めるとしたら、どこに住みたい？
If you could live in any city in America, which city would you want to live in?
