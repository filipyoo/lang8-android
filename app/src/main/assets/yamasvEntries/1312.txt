
Using ほっとく

ほっとく is a casual version of ほっておく, which means "to leave alone / to leave as is / to ignore."

その傷をほっといたら、化膿する。
- If you don't treat the wound, it'll suppurate.

この野菜はほっといても育ちます。
- This vegetable glows even if you don't take care of it at all.

私のことはほっといて。
- Leave me alone.

When you are talking about someone else in a positive sentence, you should say そっとしておく instead. ほっておく sounds as if they have no value to take care of.

彼女はまだ父親の死から立ち直ってないから、そっとしておいて。
- She hasn't recovered from the death of her father. Please let her alone.

彼女は気を引きたくて泣いているだけだからほっときなさい。
- She's crying just trying to attract your attention, so you should ignore her.
