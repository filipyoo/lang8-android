
Using もはや

When もはや is used with a negative form, it means "no longer."

君の息子はもはや子供ではない。やりたいことをやらせるべきだ。
- Your son's no longer a child; you should let him do what he wants.

このレストランはもはや以前ほど有名ではない。
- This restaurant is no longer as famous as it used to be.

この国にはもはや民主主義は存在しない。
- Democracy is no longer available in this country.


When it's used with a positive form, it means "already."

君の息子はもはや大人だ。
- Your son is already an adult.

この寿司はただの料理ではない。もはや芸術だ。
- This sushi isn't just a dish; it has already reached the status of art.

「まんが」や「かわいい」という日本語はもはや世界中で知られている。
- Japanese term "manga" or "kawaii" is already known everywhere in the world.
