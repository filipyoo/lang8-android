
Using 〜たて

When たて follows the masu stem of verbs, it means you have just done the action. 焼きたてのパン means "freshly baked bread," 産みたての卵 means "a new laid egg" and ペンキぬりたて means "wet paint."

外国語を学んでる人は覚えたての表現を使いたがる。
- Every foreign language learner wants to use an expression they've just learned.

私の家は畜産農家だから、毎朝しぼりたてのミルクが飲める。
- My family runs a livestock farm, so we can drink fresh milk from the cow every morning.

缶コーヒーは、いれたてのコーヒーほどおいしくない。
- Canned coffee doesn't taste as good as fresh brewed one.
