
Using 目を盗む

～の目を盗む means to hide from someone.

私はボスが奥さんの目を盗んで秘書と付き合っているのを知っている。
- I know my boss is seeing his secretary hiding from his wife.

高校時代は親の目を盗んでタバコを吸っていた。
- I used to smoke hiding from my parents in my high school days.


盗み見 means a surreptitious glance. You can use it as a suru-verb.

私は彼のスマホを盗み見した。
- I took a surreptitious glance at his smartphone.


盗み聞きする means to eavesdrop.

彼は私達の会話を壁越しに盗み聞きしていたようだ。
- It seems that he eavesdropped on our conversation through the wall.

盗聴 is a formal version of 盗み聞き. It's mainly used for a eavesdropping by a machine.

たくさんの人が、政府は電話の会話を全て盗聴していると思っている。
- A lot of people believe that the goverment is eavesdropping all the telephone conversation.
