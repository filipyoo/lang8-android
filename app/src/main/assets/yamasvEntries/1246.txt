
Using 左右

左 means left and 右 means right, so 左右(さゆう) means "left and right" or "both ways." If you write 右左, it's pronounced as みぎひだり. Since little children can't understand what さゆう means, you should say みぎひだり to them; ひだりみぎ sounds a little unnatural.

道路を渡る時は左右(さゆう)をよく見なさい。
道路を渡る時は右左(みぎひだり)をよく見なさい。
- You should look both ways carefully before crossing the street.


左右 is also used as a suru verb that means "to affect" or "to decide."

どれだけがんばったかが君の将来を左右する。
- How hard you work decides your future.

性能よりもデザインがスマートフォンの売上を左右する。
- The sales of smartphones are affected by the design rather than the performance.

天気がぶどうの品質を大きく左右する。
- The weather has a significant effect on the quality of grapes.
