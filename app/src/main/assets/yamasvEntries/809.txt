
Using 適当

適当 can be used to mean "appropriate," but it's usually used to mean "not accurate" or "unreliable." You should use 適切 to mean "appropriate."

彼はいつも適当に返事する。
He always responds in a nonchalant tone.
He always responds without thinking deeply.

このウェブページの情報は適当なものばかりです。
There are a lot of unreliable information on this web page.

適当に聞いてたから、彼がそのプレゼンで何を説明してたか覚えてない。
I don't remember what he explained in the presentation because I listened to it carelessly.

正確に量を測る必要はありません。適当でいいです。
You don't have to measure the amount accurately.
