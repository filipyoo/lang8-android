
Using ～とおぼしき

～とおぼしき is a very formal version of ～と思われる, which means "seems to be ～."  It modifies the noun that follows it.

刑事と思しき男が角に立っている。
- A guy who seems to be a detective is standing at the corner.

犯人と思しき男が逃げるのを見た。
- I saw a guy who seems to be the murderer running away.

彼らは盗品とおぼしき車を売っている。
- They sell cars that seems to be stolen ones.

警備員が爆弾とおぼしきものを見つけた。
- A security found something that seems to be a bomb.
