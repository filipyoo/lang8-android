
Using ～上がり

上がり can be used to describe your former job. It follows a noun in this case.

彼は軍人上がりだから喧嘩が強い。
- He was a solder, so he's a great fighter.

新しい社長は技術者上がりだから、技術に詳しい。
- Since the new president worked as an engineer before, he's familiar with technologies.


The verb 上がる is mainly used to mean "to rise" or "to go up," but it also can be used to mean "to end."

今日は仕事は何時に上がるの？
- What time will your work end today?

病気上がりだから働きたくない。
- I was just recovered from an illness, so I don't want to work.
