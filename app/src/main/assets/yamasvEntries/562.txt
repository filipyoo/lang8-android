
Using 一味違う(ひとあじちがう)

"一味違う" means "a little bit different." It has a positive nuance.

このビールは一味違う。
"This beer tastes a little bit different from others" is a literal translation. This sentence implies that "this beer tastes better than others." This type of phrase is often used in ads.

仕事の後のビールは一味違う。
- Beer after work tastes better than usual.

彼の言うことはいつも一味違う。
- His opinion is unique and interesting.

彼の演技は一味違う。
- He's a good actor. No other actors can perform like him.
