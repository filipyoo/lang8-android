
〜ようになる / 〜ようにする

Verb + ようになる has two usages.

First, it is used when you change your habitual action.

毎朝、牛乳を飲むようになりました。
Meaning: I didn't drink milk in the past, but I drink milk every morning now.

最近、タバコを吸うようになりました。
I began smoking recently.


Next, it is also used when ability or possibility has changed.

子供が歩けるようになりました。
My kid started walking lately.

日本語の本が読めるようになりました。
Meaning: I couldn't read a book in Japanese in the past, but I can read it now.

自転車を買ったので、短い時間で学校に行けるようになりました。
Meaning: It took a long time to go to school before, but it takes a short time now because I bought a bicycle. 


You can also use it for the future event.

9月に新しいiPhoneが買えるようになります。
You will be able to get a new iPhone in September.

来月から、オンラインでチケットが買えるようになります。
You will be able to buy a ticket online from next month.

You can also use it in a conditional sentence.

このソフトを使えば、無料で電話できるようになります。
Using this software, you can make free calls.


Verb + ようにする is used when you have decided to change something. Sometimes it has the nuance of "try."

毎朝、牛乳を飲むようにします。
Meaning: I didn't drink milk every morning in the past, but I try to drink milk every morning.

タバコは止めるようにします。
I try to stop smoking.

明日は８時に学校に行くようにします。
Meaning: I don't go to school at eight o'clock usually, but I will go to school at eight o'clock next morning.

You can't use ようにする for what you can't control.
x 試験に合格するようにします。(I will pass the exam.)
x 9月までに泳げるようにします。(I will be able to swim by September.)
