
Using 正直

正直(しょうじき) is used as an adjective and an adverb.

彼はとても正直です。
- He is very honest.

正直な人は、たまに損をする。
- Honesty does not always pay.

正直な意見を聞かせて。
- I'd like to know your honest opinion.
(This Japanese sentence implies that the speaker doesn't care if the opinion is inconvenient for them.)


正直、私は自分の仕事が嫌いです。
- Honestly, I hate my job.

正直、私は何も知りません。
- I honestly don't know anything.

正直、それはかなりむずかしい仕事です。
- Believe me, it's a very difficult task.
