
The Usage of 〜直す

〜直す is used when you do something which you've done before. It follows the masu stem of verbs.

日本語を完全に忘れてしまったので、勉強し直さないといけない。
- I've forgotten Japanese completely, so I need to relearn it. 

彼のレポートはひどかったので、書き直すように命令した。
- Since his report was terrible, I ordered him to rewrite it.

わからなかったので、彼に聞き直した。
- I asked him again because I didn't understand it.

食べる前に、その弁当を電子レンジで温め直したほうがいい。
- You should reheat this bento in a microwave before eating it.

彼に電話をかけ直させます。
- I'll let him call you back.
(You can say かけ直す even if someone else called you and you call them back.)
