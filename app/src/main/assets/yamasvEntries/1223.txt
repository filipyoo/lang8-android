
Using と after a time

When と follows a time, it stresses that the time is very short.

すごく簡単な手術なので、15分とかかりません。
- It's a very simple operation. It won't take more than 15 minutes.

3才の子供は5分と待てない。
- Three-year-old children can't wait even five minutes.

彼は世界中を旅行している最中で、3日と同じ場所にいない。
- He's travelling all over the world. He never stays in the same place for more than three days.
