
The Usage of 〜ぶり.


寿司は３年ぶりです。

This simple sentence in Japanese is correct and natural. It means "I ate sushi for the first time since I last ate it three years ago." 
It's a short form of "私は、寿司を食べるのは３年ぶりです." You can omit the subject "私は," and you can also omit "を食べるの" because the listener can imagine the verb with ease from the word 寿司 .

彼女とは３年ぶりです。
I met her for the first time since we met three years ago.

東京は３年ぶりです。
I have visited Tokyo for the first time since I last visited here three years ago.

飛行機は３年ぶりです。
It's my first flight since my last flight three years ago.


You can use 〜ぶりの to modify a following word.

３年ぶりの寿司です。
３年ぶりの彼女との再開です。
３年ぶりの東京です。
３年ぶりの飛行機です。
