
Using 〜やら〜やら

It's used to list examples/reasons.

主人の部屋は、ゲームやらマンガやらでいっぱいだ。まるで子供の部屋のよう。
- My husband's room is filled with video games, manga and so on. It looks like a kid's room.

暑さやら騒音やらで、昨夜は全然眠れなかった。
- What with heat and noise, I couldn't sleep at all last night.

バイトやら勉強やらで、テレビを楽しむ時間はありません。
- What with part time job and studies, I have not time to enjoy TV.

旅行者やら出張の人やらで、このホテルはいつも満室だ。
- This hotel is always full with tourists, travelers on business, and so on.


何やら means "everything." It's placed at the second position.

最近、仕事やら何やらですごく忙しい。
- Lately, I've been very busy what with work and everything.
