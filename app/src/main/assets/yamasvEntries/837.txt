
Using がかり

がかり is used to stress that you took a lot of time or resources to do something. It usually follows a number.

彼らは10年がかりでそのロボットを開発した。
They took 10 years to develop the robot.

一日がかりでこの料理を作った。
I took whole day to cook the meal.

5人がかりでこの大きな家具を動かした。
It took five people to move that big furniture.

重機3台がかりでそのビルを解体した。
It took three heavy equipments to dismantle the building.
