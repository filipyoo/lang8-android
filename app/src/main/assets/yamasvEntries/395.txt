
〜とばかり思ってた

"〜と思っていた" is used to mean "I thought that 〜." On the other hand, "〜とばかり思っていた" is used to mean "I was sure that 〜" and used when you are surprised to learn that the fact is completely different.

日本語が上手だから、あなたは日本人だとばかり思っていた。
I was sure that you were a Japanese person because your Japanese was really good.

彼のことが好きだとばかり思っていた。
I was sure that you loved him.

東京に転勤したとばかり思っていた。
I was sure that you had been transferred to Tokyo.
