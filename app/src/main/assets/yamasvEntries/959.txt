
Using 気が知れない

気が知れない is used when you can't understand what someone is thinking. It's mainly used when they do stupid things.

昼食に10,000円も払う人の気が知れない。
- I can't understand someone who pays 10,000 yen for lunch.

彼らは結婚式の10日後に離婚したらしい。彼らの気が知れない。
- I heard that they got divorced 10 days after the wedding ceremony. I can't understand what they are thinking.

彼とだけは結婚したくない。彼を好きな人の気が知れない。
- He's the last person I want to get married to. I can't understand someone who likes him.
