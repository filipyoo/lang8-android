
Using 落ち着く

落ち着く means "to calm down."

落ち着け!
- Calm down! / Don't panic!

It also means to feel relaxed.

家にいる時が一番落ち着く。
- Nothing makes me feel more relaxed than being in my house.

こんな狭いホテルの部屋じゃ落ち着けない。
- You can't feel relaxed in such a small hotel room.


～ことに落ち着く is used when a negotiation or discussion comes to a conclusion.

長い議論の結果、その製品の発売日を延期することに落ち着いた。
- After a long discussion, we decided to postpone the release date of the product.

彼らはクリントンを支持することに落ち着いたようだ。
- It seems that they agreed to support Clinton.
