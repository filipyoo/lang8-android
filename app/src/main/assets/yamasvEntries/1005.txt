
Using つきもの

When something (A) is often accompanied by something else (B), you can say "AにBはつきものだ." 

For example:

スポーツに怪我はつきものだ。
- You could be injured when you play sports.

偉大な発明に失敗はつきものだ。
- Great invention cannot be achieved without failures.

モータースポーツに危険はつきものだ。
- Motor sports are always attended with danger.

投資にリスクはつきものだ。
- You can't invest without risks.
