
Using そうすれば

そうすれば can be used to explain what does what you suggested in the previous sentence bring. It's always placed in the beginning of a sentence like "that way" in English.

外で食べる代わりに弁当を作ってきたほうがいい。そうすればお金が貯まる。
- You should bring bento instead of eating out. That way you can save money.

食事の後にいつもアイスクリームを食べるのを止めなさい。そうすれば痩せますよ。
- Stop eating ice cream after every meal. That way you can lose weight.

- Why is this alarm going off?
- Buckle up. Then it will stop.
なんでアラームが鳴ってるの？
シートベルトを締めて。そうすれば、止まる。
