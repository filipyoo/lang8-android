
Using いったい

You can use 一体(いったい) to stress a question; it's used you don't have a clue at all. You can use the kanji, but I think using hiragana is more common.

いったいどうしたの？
- What the hell is going on?

いったい自分を誰だと思ってるんだ？
- Who the hell do you think you are?
(You can't say あなた instead of the 自分 in this case.)

いったいなんであんな失礼なことを言ったんだ？
- Why on earth did you say such a rude thing?
