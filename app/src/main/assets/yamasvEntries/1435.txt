
Using 〜ほどではない

〜ほどではない means "not so much as 〜." ほどじゃない is a casual version of it.

彼は日本語を流暢に話せるけど、日本人ほどではない。
- He can speak Japanese fluently, but not as well as a native speaker.

この包丁はよく切れるけど、日本製ほどじゃない。
- This cooking knife is sharp, but not so sharp as Japanese one.


It can also follow a clause.

彼は病気だけど、歩けないほどじゃない。
- He's sick but not so weak that he can't walk.

サッカーは好きだけど、スタジアムに観に行くほどじゃない。
- I like soccer, but not so much that I go to the stadium to watch a game.

日本語は話せるけど通訳になれるほどじゃない。
- I can speak Japanese, but not enought to become an interpretter.
