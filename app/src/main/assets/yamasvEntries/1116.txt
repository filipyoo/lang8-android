
Using 礼を言う

礼を言う means "to thank" or "to say thank you."

彼は礼を言って出て行った。
- He thanked me and went out.

彼は礼も言わずに出て行った。
- He went out without even saying thank you.

礼も言えないの？
- Can't you even say thank you?

落とした財布を警察に届けてくれた人に礼を言いたいが、その人は名前を名乗らなかったそうだ。
- I'd like to say thank you to the person who turned in my lost wallet to the police, but they said that he didn't give his name.
