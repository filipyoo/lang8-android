
Using 〜と言ったら〜

"ダメと言ったらダメ!" means "if I said NO, it means NO!" 
You can use it when you want to stress that you'll never change the mind.

締め切りを明日まで伸ばしてくれませんか。
だめ、今日といったら今日だ!
- Can you please extend the deadline until tomorrow?
- No! If I said TODAY, it means TODAY!


It can follow a verb too.

日本に行くのは諦めたら？外国人が日本で仕事を探すのは大変だよ。
行くと言ったら行く!
- You should give up going to Japan. It's difficult for foreigners to find a job there.
- I'll go, whatever happens!

彼はやると言ったらやる男だ。
- He's the man who never breaks a promise.

本当にパーティに行かないの？
行かないと言ったら行かない！
- Are you sure you won't go to the party?
- I said NO!

本当に鍵を閉めた？
閉めたと言ったら閉めた！
- Are you sure that you locked the door?
- Definitely!
