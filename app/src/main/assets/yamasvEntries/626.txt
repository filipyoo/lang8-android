
Using 極まる and 極まりない

Interestingly, 極まる and 極まりない are the same. They mean "extremely" and follow a noun which modify something.

彼の態度は失礼 極まる/極まりない。
- His attitude is extremely rude.

そのプロジェクトは困難 極まった/極まりなかった。
- That project was highly difficult.

酒を飲んで運転するのは危険 極まる/極まりない。
- Drinking and driving is really dangerous.



Similarly, 嘘をつくな and 嘘をつけ are the same: "Don't lie." Both "馬鹿言うな" and "馬鹿言え" mean "Don't say stupid thing."
