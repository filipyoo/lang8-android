
Using 一方(いっぽう)

When 一方 appears at the top of the sentence, it means "on the other hand."

彼は日本語をネイティブのように流暢に話せる。一方、読むことは全然できない。
He can speak Japanese fluently like a native speaker. On the other hand, he can't read it at all.

一方 can also follow the dictionary form of verbs. Therefore, you can connect the two sentences above:
彼は日本語をネイティブのように流暢に話せる一方、読むことは全然できない。

彼はエンジニアとして働く一方、夜は学校に通っている。
While working as an engineer, he goes to school at night.

その薬はよく効く一方、副作用もある。
This medicine works very well, but it has side effects.



一方 has another usage. It can also be used when something keep going the same direction. 一方 is usually used as the predicate in this case.

物価は上がる一方だ。
Prices keep on rising.

私の借金は増える一方だ. 
My debts go on increasing.
