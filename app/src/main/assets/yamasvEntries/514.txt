
The Meaning of お客さん

You can use お客さん to mean "guests,"  "clients" and "customers."

お客さんが私の料理を気に入ってくれるといいんだけど。
- I hope my guests like my dishes.

お客さんと午後に打ち合わせがある。
- We are going to have a meeting with my clients this afternoon.

お客さんの満足度を上げないといけない。
- We have to increase customers' satisfactions.

You can also use 顧客(こきゃく) to mean "clients" and "customers."


お客さん is also a slang term which means a sports team or player who always lose to a specific team or player. 

タイガースはライオンズをお客さんにしている。
- The Lions always lose to the Tigers.
(The Tigers call the Lions as "お客さん" because the Tigers welcome the Lions.)
