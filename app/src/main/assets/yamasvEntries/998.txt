
Using 使えない

使えない is the negative potential form of 使う, which means "to use."

私は箸がうまく使えない。
- I can't use chopsticks well.

SIMカードがないとこの携帯は使えない。
- You can't use this mobile phone without SIM card.


It can be also used to mean "useless." 

彼は使えない選手だ。この10試合で1点も取っていない。
- He's a useless player. He hasn't scored even one goal in the past ten games.

この辞書は使えない。いい使い方の例があまり載っていない。
- This dictionary is useless. It doesn't have good examples to use.

The opposite of 使えない is 使える, which means "useful."

この辞書は使える。使い方のいい例がたくさん載っている。
- This dictionary is useful. It has a lot of good examples to use.

You can use 使える for people to praise them, but it may sound slightly rude because it sounds as if you regard them as a tool or something. You should say 優秀だ or できる instead.
