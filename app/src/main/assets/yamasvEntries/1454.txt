
Using 可能性

可能性 means possibility/chance.

彼が金メダルを取る可能性はない。
- There is no chance that he will win the gold medal.

日本に転勤になる可能性がある。
- There is a possibility that I am transferred to Japan.

When you are talking about a possibility of something bad, you can also say 恐れ instead. Therefore if you say 日本に転勤になる恐れがある, it implies that you don't want to be transferred to Japan.

大きな台風が私達の街を直撃する恐れがある。
- There is a possibility that a big typhoon will hit our town.

この薬は深刻な副作用をもたらす恐れがある。
- This medicine could cause a serious side effect.


When you are in a bad situation and talking about a possibility of something good, you can say 望み.

彼らはとても良いチームでみんな私たちは勝てないと思っているが、先制点を取れれば勝つ望みはある。
- They are very good team and everybody thinks that we can't beat them, but there is a chance that we will win if we can score the opening goal.

日本行きのチケットはすごく高い。宝くじにでも当たらないかぎり日本に行ける望みはない。
- The plane ticket to Japan is too expensive; there was no chance of going to Japan unless I won the lottery.
