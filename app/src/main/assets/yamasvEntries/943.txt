
Using 別に

When 別に is used by itself, it means "Nothing."

What are you doing? - 何してるの？
Nothing. - 別に。
(You can also say 何も instead in this case.)


You look depressed. What's wrong? - 元気ないね。どうかしたの？
Nothing. - 別に。


Do you have any plan for tonight? - 今夜、予定ある？
Nothing. - 別に。
(You can also say 特に instead in this case.)
