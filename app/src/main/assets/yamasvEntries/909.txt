
Using なんとか

なんとか means "to manage to" / "somehow."

なんとかそのテストに受かった。
- I managed to pass the exam.

なんとか日曜日までに家賃を払わないといけない。
- Somehow I have to pay the rent by Sunday.


It's also used when you can't remember someone's or something's name.

田中なんとかという人から電話がありました。
- There was a call from Tanaka something to you.

人事部長のなんとかさんが捜してたよ。
- The manager of personnel department, what's his name, was looking for you.
