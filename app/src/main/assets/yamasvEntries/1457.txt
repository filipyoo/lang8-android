
Using 使える

使える is the potential form of 使う, which means to use.

このアプリはアンドロイドでは使えません。
- You can't use this app on Android.

この電話は地球上のどこでも使えます。
- You can use this telephone everywhere on the earth.


使える can also be used to mean useful. 

この包丁は使える。扱いやすいし、なんでも簡単に切れる。
- This kitchen knife comes in handy. It's easy to use and can cut everything easily.

このアプリはとても使える。50ドル払った価値があった。
- This app is very useful. It was worth to pay 50 dollars.


使える can also be used for a person. 彼は使える means that he's useful or smart, but it could sound rude because it sounds as if you treat him as a tool.
