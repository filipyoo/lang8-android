
Using ～とかで

～とかで is used to describe a reason of something you heard.

大きな台風が近づいているとかで、決勝戦は延期されたらしい。
- They say that the final was postponed because a big typhoon was coming.

彼は車を盗んだとかで逮捕されたらしい。
- I heard that he was arrested because he stole a car.

息子が病気だとかで、彼女はパーティに来れないと言ってた。
- She said that she won't come to the party because her son was sick.

道が混んでるとかで、彼は遅れると言ってた。
- He said he would be late due to a traffic jam.
