Using あいにく

あいにく is an adverb that means "unfortunately."

あいにくiPhone7の在庫はありません。
- Unfortunately, we don't have stock of iPhone 7.

あいにく日本を訪れたときには桜は散っていた。
- Unfortunately, the cherry blossoms had fallen when we visited Japan.

あいにくそのパーティには行けません。
- I'm sorry, but I can't come to the party.

友達からオリンピックの開会式のチケットをもらったけど、あいにくその日は大事なテストの日だ。
- A friend of mine gave me a ticket to the Olympics opening ceremony, but unluckily it's the day of important test.


あいにくの can modify a noun that follows it.

今日決勝戦が行われたがあいにくの天気だった。
- The final match was held today, but the weather was bad.

一生懸命勉強してきたが、あいにくの結果だった。
- I had studied hard, but it didn't bring a good result.
