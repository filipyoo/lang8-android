
Using 〜顔負け

〜顔負け means to put someone to shame. It's used when you are good at something, and it follows someone who is supposed to be very good at it.

妻の料理はプロ顔負けだ。
- My wife's cooking skills put to shame a professional cook.

彼の英語の発音はネイティブ顔負けです。
- His English pronunciation is good at as well as native speakers'.

この子の歴史の知識は大人顔負けだ。
- This kid puts adults to shame about his knowledge about history.

彼女の力の強さは男顔負けだ。
- Her physical strength puts all men to shame.
