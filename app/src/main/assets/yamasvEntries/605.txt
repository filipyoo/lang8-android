
Using ある意味

ある意味 means "in a way / in a sense." It's used when something is true from a certain point of view.


彼の言うことはある意味正しい。
- What he said was true in a sense.

ある意味、貧乏なことは幸せなことだと言える。
- I can say that to be poor is, in a sense, to be happy.

漫画によく出てくるおにぎりは、ある意味日本で一番有名な食べ物かもしれません。
In a way, rice ball, which often appears in manga, may be the most popular food in Japan. 

失敗はある意味いいことだ。
- Failure could be good for you.

大金を稼ぐ事は、少しのお金を稼ぐよりある意味簡単だ。
- Making a lot of money is easier than making a little bit money in a way.
