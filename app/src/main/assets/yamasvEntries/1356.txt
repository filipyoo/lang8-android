
Using 面白い

面白い means "funny."

このマンガはとても面白かった。笑いが止まらなかった。
- This manga was very funny. I couldn't stop laughing.

彼のスピーチはとても面白い。彼は人の笑わせ方をよく知っている。
- His speech is very funny. He knows well how to make people laugh.


It also means "interesting."

このマンガはとても面白かった。忍者について深く学べる。
- This manga was very interesting. You can learn about ninja deeply. 

日本語は英語と完璧に異なるから面白いと思う。
- I'm very interested in Japanese because it's completely different from English.


It could also mean "to enjoy."

このマンガは本当に面白かった。
- I really enjoyed this manga. 

パーティは面白かった？
- Did you enjoy the party?
