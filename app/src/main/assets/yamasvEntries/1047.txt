
Using 〜にしちゃ

It's a casual version of にしては, used when something is different from what you expected. 

7月にしちゃ寒い。
- It's cold for July. 

彼は40にしちゃ若い。
- He looks young for 40.

この値段にしちゃおいしい。
- This is delicious considering the price.


It can also follow a clause.

日本に留学してたにしちゃ、君の日本語はうまくない。
- Your Japanese isn't good considering you studied in Japan.

妻が作ったにしちゃ、美味しい。本当に彼女が作ったのか？
- This is delicious considering it was cooked by my wife. I wonder if she really cooked it.

来月結婚するにしちゃ、彼女はそんなに嬉しそうに見えない。
- Although she's going to get married next month, she doesn't look so happy.
