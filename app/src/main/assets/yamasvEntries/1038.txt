
Using 乗る

乗る can be used for almost all vehicles. 

タクシーに乗る - to get in a taxi.
バスに乗る - to get on a bus.
飛行機に乗る - to board a plane.
バイクに乗る - to ride a motorcycle. 
(You can also say 馬に乗る to mean "to ride a horse.)


"乗った" can be used to mean "I'm in."

日曜日にバーベキューパーティやるけど、来る？
うん、乗った。
- We are going to have a barbecue party this Sunday. Will you come?
- I'm in.




調子に乗る has two different meanings: negative one and positive one.

1. A negative one means "to be carried away."

調子に乗るな
- Don't be carried away.

彼は成功してから調子に乗っている。
- He got carried away after his success.

彼は市長の息子だからといって調子に乗っている。
- He has a big head merely because he's the son of the mayor.

2. A positive one means "to keep a good condition / to be showing a good performance." It's often used for an athlete.

彼は最近調子に乗っているから、次のレースで世界記録を出すかもしれない。
- He's showing a good performance these days. He could establish new world record in the next race.
