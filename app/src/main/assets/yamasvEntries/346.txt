
The Usage of 限る

限る means "to limit."

パーティに参加できるのは、招待者に限る。( 限られる is also correct in this sentence. )
Only invited people can attend this party.

You can also use 限り as an adverb or a noun.

今日に限り、コーヒー一杯が無料です。
Only today, you can drink a cup of coffee for free.

スペースに限りがあるため、全員この部屋に入ることはできません。
Not All of you can enter this room due to limited space.


You can also use 限る when you recommend something strongly.

美味しい寿司を食べたいなら、この店に限る。
If you want to eat very delicious sushi, this is the only restaurant to visit.

外国語をマスターしたいなら、ネイティブスピーカーと友達になるに限る。
If you want to master a foreign language, the best way is to make a friend who is a native speaker of the language.

朝食は、妻が作ってくれた味噌汁に限る。
There's nothing like miso soup made by my wife for breakfast.
