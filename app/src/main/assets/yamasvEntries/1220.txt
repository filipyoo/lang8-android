
Using 一目

一目(ひとめ) means "at first sight."

私は一目でそのアニメに魅了された。
- I was fascinated by the anime at first sight.

私は彼女に一目ぼれした。
- I felt in love with her at first sight.


一目(いちもく)置く means to admit that someone's ability is good or to show respect to someone who has great ability.

彼はわがままなので、みんなから嫌われているが、彼の能力にはみんな一目置いている。
- Everyone doesn't like him because he's selfish, but they show respect to his ability.

ロバート・デ・ニーロですら、その子役には一目置いている。
- Even Robert De Niro admits that the child actor is gifted.
