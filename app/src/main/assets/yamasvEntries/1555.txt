
Using 〜たきり

The ta-form of a verb + きり is used when you haven't done something or a situation hasn't changed for a while.

彼とは2年前に会ったきりです。
- I haven't seen him since we met two years ago.

10ヶ月前に髪を切ったきりです。
- I haven't had my hair cut for 10 months.

このシャツは買ったきり着ていない。
- I haven't worn this shirt since I bought it. 

交通事故にあってから彼は入院したきりです。
- He has been in the hospital since he had a traffic accident.

彼は3年前に家を出たきりです。
- He left home three years ago and has never come back since then.
