
The Usage of "〜なきゃ/〜くちゃ"

"〜なきゃいけない" and "〜くちゃいけない" are casual expression to mean "have to / should." いけない is often omitted in conversation.

明日は朝早いから、いつもより早く 寝なきゃ/寝なくちゃ。
I should go to bed earlier than usual because I have to get up early next morning.

日本に留学したければ、もっとがんばって 勉強しなきゃ/勉強しなくちゃ。
You should study harder if you want to study in Japan.

3ヶ月で5キロ太った。食べ過ぎるのを 止めなきゃ/止めなくちゃ。
I have gained five kilograms in the last three months. I have to stop eating too much.

早く家に帰って、好きなTV番組を 見なきゃ/見なくちゃ、
I need to go home soon and watch my favorite TV show.
