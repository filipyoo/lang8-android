
Using と言うことは

と言うことは is used to describe something you said before from another point of view. It's interchangeable with つまり.

大統領を3期以上務めることはできない。と言うことはオバマはもう大統領に立候補できない。
- You can't serve over two terms as the president, which means Obama can't run for president again.

空港まで2時間かかる。と言うことは9時に空港に着くには7時に家をでないといけない。
- It takes two hours to the airport, which means that we have to leave home at seven to arrive there at nine.

近い将来タクシーはすべて自動運転車に置き換わる。
と言うことはタクシーの運転手はみんな仕事を失うってこと？
- All taxis will be replaced by self-driving cars in the near future.
- So you mean that all taxi drivers will lose their job?
