
The Usage of 〜ないと

"〜ないといけない (have to / should 〜)" is often shortened as "〜ないと" in casual situations.

ほら、早くしないと。
Hey, you should hurry up.

家に帰って勉強しないと。
I have to go home and study.

来週までにこの仕事を終わらせないと。
I have to finish this work by next week.

来年日本に留学するので、もっと日本語を上達させないと。
Since I'm going to study in Japan next year, I should improve my Japanese.
