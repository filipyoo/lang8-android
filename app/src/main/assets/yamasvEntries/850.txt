
Using 一度は and 一度に

When は follows a number, it means "at least." Therefore, 一度は means "at least once."

一度は高級店で高い寿司を食べてみたい。
- I'd like to try expensive sushi at a high class restaurant at least once.

週に一度はそのバーに行く。
- I go to the bar at least once a week.


一度に means "at once / at a time / at the same time."

一度にたくさん食べちゃだめ!
- Don't eat too much at once!

このエレベータは一度に20人乗れる。
- This elevator can carry 20 persons at a time.

一度に２つのことをしようとしてはいけません。
- Don't try to do two things at the same time.
