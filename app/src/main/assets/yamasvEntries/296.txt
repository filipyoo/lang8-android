
Expressions Used To Confess Secrets

1. 実は
実は is used when you confess both serious and light secrets.

実は、一ヶ月で5キロ太った.
Actually, I gained 5 kilos in one month.

実は、私は会社を辞めるつもりです。
To tell the truth, I'm going to quit this company.


2. 本当のことを言うと
本当のことを言うと is used when you dare to tell the truth.

本当のことを言うと、私は末期ガンです。
To tell the truth,  I have terminal cancer.

本当のことを言うと、私は上司が嫌いです。
To tell the truth,  I hate my boss.


3. ここだけの話だけど
ここだけの話だけど means "between you and me." It's used to confess your secret to your close and reliable person. Of course, you can't tell the story to someone else when the speaker use this expression.

ここだけの話だけど、夫を愛していない.
Just between you and me, I don't love my husband.

ここだけの話だけど、私は刑務所にいたことがある.
Just between you and me, I have been to prison before.


4. 何を隠そう
何を隠そう is used to open your secret. It's used when the speaker isn't going to keep the story secret any longer. It's often used to surprise the listener.

何を隠そう、私は彼女を愛している。
To be frank with you, I love her.

何を隠そう、私は大統領の息子です。
To tell the truth, I'm a son of the president.
