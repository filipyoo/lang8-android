
Using 腕

腕 means an arm. (Japanese people often say 手 to mean both a hand and an arm.)

氷ですべって腕を骨折した。
- I broke my arm by slipping on ice.


It's also often used as a metaphor of skills.

彼の料理の腕は一流だ。
- His cooking skill is the best.

ずっと運転していないから、腕が落ちた。
- My driving skill has declined because I haven't driven in a long time.

プログラミングの腕には自信がある。
- I have confidence in my programming skills.
