
Using 〜も〜ないで

〜も〜ないで means "even without 〜ing." It's used when you didn't do something you were supposed to do. You can also say ずに instead of ないで. ずに sounds a little more formal.

彼はさよならも 言わないで / 言わずに 去っていった。
- He left me even without saying goodbye.

彼は顔も 洗わないで / 洗わずに 出て行った。
- He went out even without washing his face.

調べも しないで / せずに ,なんでそんなことが言えるの?
- How could you say that even without checking it out?

この村はとても安全だし、皆顔見知りだから、普段は鍵も 掛けないで / 掛けずに 外出する。
- Since this village is very safe and we know each other, we usually go out even without locking the door.

昨夜、一睡も しないで / せずに その本を読んだ。
- I read the book last night without a wink of sleep.
