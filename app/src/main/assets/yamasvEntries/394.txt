
The Usage "〜くらい" and "〜ほど" to Show Examples

You can use "〜くらい" and "〜ほど" to show examples. They are used to stress the facts which will appear after them.

彼は、このホテルを買えるくらい金持ちです。
He is rich enough to buy this hotel.

このバッグは小さい女の子でも持てるくらい軽い。
The bag is light enough for a little girl to carry.

私は、毎年アメリカにワールドシリーズを見に行くくらい野球が好きです。
I like baseball so much that I go to America to watch World Series every year.

私は、話もしたくないくらい疲れてます。
I'm so tired that I don't want to talk.
 
彼の家は、庭で野球ができるほど広いです。
His place is so big that you can play baseball in his garden.

You can interchange くらい and ほど each other in all of the above Japanese sentences.
