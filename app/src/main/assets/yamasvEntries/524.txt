
顔を合わせる

"顔を合わせる" means "to see each other."

彼とは毎日学校で顔を合わせている。
- I see him at school every day.

そのバンドのメンバーが今日10年ぶりに顔を合わせる。
- All members of the band will gather today for the first time in ten years.


"面と向かう" means "face to face."

君と彼女は面と向かって話し合った方がいい。
- You and she should discuss it face to face.

彼には、上司に面と向かってそれを言う勇気がない。
- He doesn't have the guts to say it to his boss's face.


"膝を突き合わせる" and "膝を交える" are idioms which means that two people sit down very close as if their knees touch each other. They imply that two people have a frank heart-to-heart talk.
"腹を割って話す" also means "to have a heart-to-heart talk."

我々は膝を交えて話し合った。
我々は腹を割って話し合った。
- We had a frank heart-to-heart talk.

私には、腹を割って話せる友だちがいない。
- I have no friend I can talk frankly with.

もっと腹を割って話して欲しい。
- I want you to talk to me more frankly.
