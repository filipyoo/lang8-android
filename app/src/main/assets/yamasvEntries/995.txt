
Using 仕方がない

仕方がない means "it can't be helped." 

今月は雨の日が多かった。工事が遅れたのは仕方がない。
- We have a lot of rainy days this month. It can't be helped that the construction was delayed.

僕のコンピューターはすごく遅い。10年前に買ったやつだから仕方がない。
- My computer is very slow. It can't be helped because I bought it 10 years ago.


It can be also used to mean "unbearably/unbelievably." It follows the te-form of adjectives in this case.

寒くて仕方がなかったから一日中家にいた。
- I stayed at home all day because it was unbearably cold.

一人暮らしを始めた時、さびしくて仕方がなかったから毎日家族に電話をしていた。
- When I started living alone, I phoned my family every day because I felt unbearably lonely.

子犬がかわいくて仕方がないから、一人で家に残したくない。
- My puppy is unbelievably cute. I don't want to leave her home alone.
(匹 is used as the counter for animals in general, but you can count your pets as though they are human beings if you love them very much.)


You can also use しょうがない instead of 仕方がない。
