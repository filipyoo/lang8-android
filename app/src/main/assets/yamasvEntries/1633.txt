
Using 〜きり and 〜以来

〜きり is used when you haven't done something since an event. It follows the ta-form of a verb.

彼とは大学を卒業したきり会っていない。
彼とは大学を卒業したきりだ。
- I haven't seen him since we graduated from college.
You can omit 会っていない because it's implied.

スーパーファミコンは子供の頃遊んだきりだ。
- I haven't played Super Famicom since I was a kid.

トム・クルーズの映画はトップガンを観たきりだ。
- I haven't seen a Tom Cruise movie since "Top Gun."


〜以来 follows the te-form of a verb and is also used in the same situation, but it implies that you do something you haven't done for a long time.

彼と会うのは大学を卒業して以来だ。
彼とは大学を卒業して以来だ。
- I'm meeting him for the first time since we graduated from college.
You can omit 会う because it's implied.

スーパーファミコンは子供の時以来だ。
- I'm playing Super Famicom for the first time since I was a kid.

昨夜ミッション・インポッシブルを観た。トム・クルーズの映画はトップガン以来だ。
- I saw ”Mission Impossible" last night; It was the first time I saw a Tom Cruise movie since "Top Gun."
