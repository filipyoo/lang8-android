
Using みるみる

みるみる is an adverb used when something has changed in a short time.

ジムに行くのをやめてからみるみる太った。
- I've gained weight in a short time since I quit going to the gym.

去年日本に引っ越してから君の日本語はみるみる上達した。
- Your Japanese has improved in a short time after you moved to Japan last year.

暑さのせいで氷の彫刻はみるみる解けた。
- Because of the hot weather, the ice sculpture melted very fast.
