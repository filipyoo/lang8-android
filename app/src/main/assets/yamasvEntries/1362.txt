
Using 頃

When 頃(ごろ) follows a time, it means "around."

日本のサクラは４月頃咲きます。
- Cherry blossoms in Japan bloom around April.

私は毎朝7時頃仕事に行きます。
- I go to work around seven o'clock every morning.

It also can follow the masu stem of verbs, mainly 見る, 飲む and 食べる, to mean "the best time/season of doing the action."

サクラは今週末が見頃です。
- This weekend will be the best time to enjoy cherry blossoms.

そのワインの飲み頃はいつですか？
- When is the best time to drink the wine?

この果物の食べごろは9月頃です。
- The best time to eat this fruit is around September.
