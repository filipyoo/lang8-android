
Using 〜にも増して

〜にも増して means "much more than."

今日は昨日にも増して暑い。
- It's much hotter than yesterday.
(This sentence implies that it was very hot yesterday too. If you merely say "今日は昨日より暑い," it doesn't tell you whether it was hot yesterday or not.)

君は以前にも増してきれいになった。
- You are much more beautiful than before.

人生において何にも増して健康が大事だ。
- Nothing is more important than health in life.

誰にも増して君が好きだ。
- I like you more than anybody else.

漫画のことになると彼は誰にも増して饒舌になる。
- When it comes to manga, he becomes more talkative than anybody else.
