
Using 一時

一時(いちじ) has two meanings: one o'clock or temporarily (or "for a while").

一時に空港に着いた。
- I arrived at the airport at one o'clock.

その試合は雨で一時中断した。
- The game was suspended temporarily because of the rain.

私は一時東京に住んでいた。
- I lived in Tokyo for a while.

この国は一時スペインの植民地だった。
- This country was a colony of Spain for a while.

When it's used to mean "for a while," it can also be pronounced as いっとき.
