
Using こそ

こそ is used to stress the subject it follows.

この本こそ私がずっと探していた本だ。
- This is the very book I've been looking for.

今年こそ優勝する。
- This is the year we're going to win the championship.

彼こそ辞めるべきだ。
- He's the one who should resign.

素晴らしい! これこそ技術だ。
- It's amazing! This is THE technology.

これこそ社長に求められているものだ。
- This is exactly what the president is expected to do.
