
Using もってこい

もってこい is used when something or someone is ideal/appropriate for a purpose. に or には follows the object.

この本は日本語の勉強にもってこいだ。やさしい日本語で書かれているし、すごく面白い。
- This book is ideal for studying Japanese. It's written by easy Japanese and very interesting.

彼はうちの会社の社長にもってこいだ。
- He is the right person for the president of our company.

この車は都会に住む女性にもってこいだ。コンパクトで運転しやすい。
- This car is ideal for women who live in a big city. It's compact and easy to drive.

ここは住むには持ってこいの場所だ。静かだし、会社からもそんなに遠くない。
- This is the best place to live. It's calm and not so far from my office.
