
Using 〜もどき and 〜ばり

〜もどき is used for something that looks like something else. It has slightly negative nuance; it implies something is fake.

この寿司もどきの食べ物は何?
- What's this food looks like sushi?

この会社はiPhoneもどきのスマホを売っている。
- This company sells smartphone looks like an iPhone. 


〜ばり is used when someone does something like someone else.

あの少年はメッシばりのドリブルをする。
- That boy can dribble like Messi.

彼はスティーブ・ジョブスばりの自信家だ。
- He's a self-confident person like Steve Jobs.
