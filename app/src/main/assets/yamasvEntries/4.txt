
The Usage of "より"

This is a normal usage of "より"
私は母より背が高い。  -  I am taller than my mother.

You can use these expressions:
より良い製品を作りたい。
成績がより悪くなった。
In these examples, an object to compare is omitted. The former means "I want to create better products than the current one. "
The latter means "I got bad results than before."  
You can omit an object to compare when you use "より", if it is clear.
