
The Usage of 〜まくる

まくる follows the i-form of verbs and means "to do something many times / incessantly." It has the nuance of "indiscriminately" or "irrationally."


日本に行ったら、気が済むまで寿司を食べまくりたい。
I'd like to eat a lot of sushi until I'm happy when I visit Japan.

彼女と一緒にランチを食べると、いつも一人でしゃべりまくる。
She always monopolizes the conversation whenever we have lunch together.

日本語を上達させるために、先生に質問しまくるつもりです。
I'm going to ask questions to my Japanese teacher again and again in order to improve my Japanese.

彼はパーティの間中、新車について話しまくっていた。
He went on about his new car during the party.

日本を訪れる観光客の何割かは、秋葉原で電化製品を買いまくる。
Some tourists who visit Japan spend a lot of money on appliances in Akihabara.

高校の頃は遊びまくっていた。もっと一生懸命勉強すべきだった。
I played as much as I want when I was in high school. I should have studied harder.
