
Using 目に見えて

目に見えて is used when something has changed dramatically.

炭酸を飲むのを止めて運動を始めてから目に見えて痩せた。
- I've lost weight dramatically since I had stopped drinking soda and started exercise.

最近海外からの旅行者が目に見えて増えている。
- Lately, the number of travelers from overseas has been apparently increasing.

私が大金持ちの娘だと知って彼の態度が目に見えて変わった。彼は信用できないと思う。
- His attitude to me has apparently changed since he knew that I'm a daughter of a billionaire. I don't think I should trust him.
