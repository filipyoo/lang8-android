
The Usage of 〜ておく

〜ておく has two usages.

First, it's used when you do something in advance for some purposes.

彼が午後に来るので、午前中にケーキを焼いておく。
Since he is supposed to visit my house this afternoon, I'll bake a cake before noon.

ビールを冷やしておきます。
I'll keep the beer cool in the fridge for you.

猫が家に入れるように、窓を開けておいた。
I opened the window so that my cat can get into my house.

道に迷わないようにホテルまでのルートを調べておきなさい。
Let's check the route to the hotel to avoid losing my way.


Second, it's used when you keep the current status.

すごく暑かったので、昨日の晩は窓を開けておいた。
I kept the windows open last night because it was very hot.

自転車を夜、外に置いておかないで。盗まれるよ。
Don't leave your bicycle outside at night. It could be stolen.

これはすごく高いものだから、金庫にしまっておいて。
It's a very expensive one, so keep it in the safe. 

近いうちに必要になるかもしれないから、これはとっておく。
I'll keep it. It might become necessary in the near future.
