
Using 細かい

When you are talking about a size, 細かい(こまかい) means "very small."

私の車には細かい傷がたくさんある。
- There are a lot of very small scratches on my car.

最近新聞の細かい文字が読めない。
- I can't read fine characters in a newspaper these days.

細かく is an adverb.

この包丁は野菜を細かく刻むのに向いている。
- This kitchen knife is suitable for hashing vegetables.

細かいお金 means small change. If you have only 10,000 yen bill when you buy something that costs 100 yen, you can say "細かいのがなくてすみません" to mean "Sorry, I don't have small change." The の is a pronoun that refers お金.


細かい is also used to mean "detailed."

このカタログにはその車の細かい情報が載っている。
- This catalog describes detailed information about the car.

彼はその薬について細かく説明してくれた。
- He gave us a detailed explanation about the drug.
