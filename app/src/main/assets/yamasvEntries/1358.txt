
Expressions in Relationship

告白する means to ask him/her out. 

彼女に告白するつもりだ。/ 彼女に好きだと言うつもりだ。
- I'm going to ask her out. / I'm going to tell her I love her.

告る(こくる) is a slang; it's a short version of 告白する.

彼女に告ったけど無視された。
- I asked her out, but she ignored it.


付き合う means to see each other or to go out.

どれくらい付き合っているんですか?
- How long have you been seeing each other?

デキている is a slang meaning to see each other.

あの二人はデキていると思う。
- I think that two guys are seeing each other.


くっつく means to stick to something.

チューインガムがドアノブにくっついている。
- A chewing gum sticks to the door knob.

It's also used as a slang meaning "to start to see each other."

ケンとジュンがくっついたらしい
- They say that Ken and Jun started to see each other.


別れる means to break up. 離れる is a slang meaning it.

ケンとジュンは別れたらしい。/ ケンとジュンは離れたらしい。
- They say that Ken and Jun broke up.

振る means to break up with 〜.

ジュンがケンを振ったらしい。
- They say that Jun broke up with Ken.

彼女に振られた。
- She broke up with me.


ヨリを戻す means to get back together.

ケンとジュンはヨリを戻したらしい。
- They say that Ken and Jun got back together.


二股をかける means two-timing.

彼女は彼が二股をかけていることに気づいた。
- She found out that he was two-timing her.
