
The Difference Between 拒否 and 辞退

Both 拒否 and 辞退 are translated as "to refuse." 辞退 is used when the object is supposed to be a good thing for the subject.

家族と一緒に過ごす時間が必要なので、管理職への昇進は辞退した。
- I refused the promotion to a manager because I need time with my family.

- 彼はノーベル賞を辞退した最初の人物です。
He was the first person who refused the Nobel prize.

すみませんが、その招待は辞退させてください。
- I'm sorry, but I have no choice but to decline the invitation. 


If not, you should use 拒否.

彼は謝罪を拒否した。
- He refused to apologize. 

彼は宗教上の理由で手術を拒否した。
- He refused to have an operation for religious reasons.
