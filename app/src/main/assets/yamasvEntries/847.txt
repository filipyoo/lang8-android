
Using 〜並み

並み is used when you are on the level of something. It follows a noun.

姉の料理はプロ並みです。
- My sister's cooking is professional.

彼の日本語はネイティブ並みです。
- He can speak Japanese fluently like a native.

今日は真夏並みに暑い。
- It's hot like the middle of summer today.


It can be used in a negative sentence.

あの選手は素人並みだ。そのうちクビになるだろう。
- That player is on the level of amature. He'll be fired in the near future.
