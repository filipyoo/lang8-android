
Using 余裕

余裕 is a noun that means "margin" or "time to spare."

私の部屋はすごく狭いから、このベッドを置く余裕はない。
- My room is too small; There is no enough space for the bed.

今日はすごく忙しいから外にランチを食べに行く余裕はない。
- I'm very busy today, I can't go out for lunch with you.

余裕がある is also used to mean to afford.

新しい車を買う余裕はない。
- I can't afford to buy a new car.

余裕 is also used to express that something is easy in a casual conversation.

日本に住めば日本語は余裕で覚えられると思っていた。
- I thought I was able to master Japanese with ease if I lived in Japan.

10日あれば、その仕事は余裕で終わる。
- Ten days is enough time to finish the job.

腕立て伏せ20回できる？
余裕。
- Can you do 20 push ups?
- It's a piece of cake.
