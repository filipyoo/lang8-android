
Using おのずと

おのずと is an adverb that means "naturally/spontaneously." It sounds more formal than 自然と.

日本に住むとおのずと日本語はうまくなる。
- Your Japanese would improve spontaneously if you lived in Japan.

年をとるとおのずと友達は減る。
- The older you get, the less friends you have.

この街に生まれた少年はおのずとヤンキースのファンになる。
- Every boy who was born in this city becomes a fan of the Yankees.

このマンガを読むとおのずと忍者に興味を持つ。
- Everyone who reads this manga comes to be interested in ninja.
