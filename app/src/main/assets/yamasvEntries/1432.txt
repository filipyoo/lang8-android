
Using 回す(まわす)

車を回す means to pull/bring/send a car around where the listener is.

車を回してきます。
- I'll go and bring my car around.

事務所に車を回します。
- I'll send a car around your office.

もういかないといけません。タクシーを回してくれませんか?
- I have to go. Can you call me a taxi?


回す is also used to mean to send an invoice, notification or something like that.

請求書は彼に回しておきます。
- I'll send him the invoice.

彼の結婚パーティの通知がメールで回ってきた。
- I received an email that notice of his wedding party.


It can also be used for people.

警官をもう何人かそちらに回します。
- I'll send more police officers there.

大阪支店が人手不足なので、我々の何人かがそちらに回される。
- Osaka branch is understaffed, so some of us are being transferred there.
