
Using 付き合う(つきあう)

付き合う is used to mean "to be dating."

僕は日本人の女の子と付き合っている。
I'm dating with a Japanese girl.


It's also used when people or organizations associate with other people or organizations.

彼と私は20年以上前から友達として付き合っている。
- He and I are friends since over 20 years ago.

悪い奴と付き合うな。
- Don't keep company with bad guys.

私達の会社は、トヨタやソニーのような日本の大企業と付き合ってます。
- Our company has a business with a lot of Japanese big companies like Toyota and Sony.


You can also use it when you do something with someone else. 〜につきあう follows a noun.

買い物に付き合ってくれる?
- Can you go shopping with me?

午後は、クライアントのゴルフに付き合わないといけない。
- I have to go golfing with my clients this afternoon.

ビールに付き合わない?
- Would you like to drink beer with me?
