
Using 心強い

心強い(こころづよい) means "reassuring."

私は少し危険な地域に住んでいるけど、父も兄も警察官です。
それは心強い。
- I live in a little dangerous area, but my dad and brother are cops.
- That's reassuring.

私は日本語が話せないから、日本に行くのに日本語が話せるあなたがいると心強い。
- I don't speak Japanese, so being with you, who speaks Japanese, is very reassuring to visit Japan.


The opposite of it is 心細い(こころぼそい), not 心弱い.

私は日本語が話せないから、１人で日本に行くのは少し心細い。
- I don't speak Japanese, so going Japan by myself makes me feel a little helpless.


It's also used when you don't have enough money to do something.

二週間東京に滞在するのに10万円では心細い。
- A hundred thousand yen isn't enough to stay in Tokyo for two weeks.
