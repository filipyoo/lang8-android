
Using まず with a Negative Form

You can use まず with a negative form when the possibility is very low.

この地方は、雪はまず降らない。
It hardly snows this area.

明日の試合はまず勝てないだろう。
I don't think we will be able to win the game tomorrow.

歩いて行くとまず間に合わないだろう。
You can't be on time if you walk.

彼が金メダルを取ると予想した人はまずいなかったと思う。
I think that no one expected that he would win the gold medal.
