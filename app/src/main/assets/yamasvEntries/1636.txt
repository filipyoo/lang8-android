
Using うるさい

うるさい means "noisy" or "loud."

隣の部屋がすごくうるさい。
- The room next door is very noisy.

うるさい音楽は嫌いだ。
- I don't like loud music.


It's also used when you are particular/picky about something.

ブラジル人はコーヒーにうるさい。
- Brazilian people are particular about coffee.

僕の英語の先生は文法にうるさい。
- My English teacher is picky about Grammar.
