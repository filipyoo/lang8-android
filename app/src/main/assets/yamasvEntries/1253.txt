
Using 頼む

頼む means to ask or order.

彼に手伝ってくれるように頼もう。
- I'll ask him to help us.

ビールとピザを頼んだ。
- I ordered beer and pizza.


頼む is also used to mean "please." It's mainly used when you ask for something desperately. 

お金を少し貸してくれ。頼む。
- Lend me some money, please.

2,3日待ってください。頼みます。
- Please wait for a few days.


頼む is also used to mean to take care of something.

休みの間、犬を頼みます。
- Take care of my dog during the vacation. Thanks.



頼んだよ can be used to mean "I'm counting on you."

金を貸してくれるように銀行を説得してくれよ。頼んだよ。
- Please persuade the bank to lend us money. We're counting on you.

何があってもこの仕事は金曜日までに終わらせてくれ。頼んだよ。
- Finish this job by Friday no matter happens. I'm depending on you.


It's also used to make sure something.

二度と遅刻しないように。頼んだよ。
- Make sure not to be late again. Do you understand?
