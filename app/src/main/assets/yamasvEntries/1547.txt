
Using ～ような人

ような人 follows a phrase to mean the kind of a person who ～.

約束を守らないような人とは働きたくない。
- I don't want to work with the kind of a person who doesn't keep a promise.

誕生日に100本のバラをプレセントしてくれるような人と結婚したい。
- I hope to marry the kind of a guy who gave me a hundred of roses on my birthday.

You can use ～ような for a thing or area.

3秒で時速100Kmに届くような車が欲しい。
- I want the kind of a car that can reach 100 km/h in three seconds.

強盗の心配がないような国に住みたい。
- I want to live in the kind of a country where you don't have to worry about robbery.

これはあなたの人生を変えるようなアプリです。
- This is the kind of an app that makes your life change.
