
Using 〜まで

〜まで is mainly used to mean "to 〜" or "until 〜."

駅まで連れて行きます - I'll take you to the station.
日曜日までここにいます - I'm going to stay here until Sunday.


〜まで is also used to stress a noun.

財布だけでなくパスポートまで失くした。
- I lost not only my wallet but also my passport.
("パスポートだけでなく財布まで失くした" sounds a little unnatural because your passport is more important for you than your wallet.)

あなたまでそんなこと言うなんて信じられない。友達だと思ってた。
- I can't believe that even you say such things. I thought we were friends.

仕事を辞めて、彼は自信まで失くしたようだ。
- He seems to lose even a confidence after he quitted his job.

両親まで僕に嘘をついていた。
- Even my parents lied to me.
