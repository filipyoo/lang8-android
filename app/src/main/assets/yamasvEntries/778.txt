
"10%に減らす" and "10%減らす"

"10%に減らす" means "to reduce TO 10 percent." "10%減らす" means "to reduce BY 10 percent;" you don't have to add any particle after a number in this case.


法人税は30%に引き下げられます。
- The corporate tax will be reduced to 30 percent.

その自転車は、以前は2万円で売られていた。今は10%値下げして1万8千円で売られている。
- The bike was sold at 20,000 yen before. They discount it by 10 percent; it's sold at 18,000 yen now.

今日注文するなら、3千円値引きします。
- If you order it today, the price will be reduced by 3,000 yen.

今日注文するなら、3千円に値引きします。
- If you order it today, the price will be reduced to 3,000 yen.


Of course, this rule is the same as when it increases.

この国の人口は、2030年までに30%増えるだろう。
The population of this country will increase by 30 percent by 2030.

消費税は去年15%に引き上げられた。
-The sales tax rate was raised to 15 percent last year.
