
Expressions Using 首

首を横に振る means "to shake your head," which also means "to say no."

いくら金を積んでも彼は首を横に振った。
-  He said no, no matter how much money I offered.


首を縦に振る means "to nod your head," which also means "to say yes."

マンガのコレクションをあげると言ったらやっと彼は首を縦に振った。
- Finally, he said yes when I offered him my manga collection.


首をかしげる means "to put your head on one side," which also means "to wonder why."

ニュートンはなぜリンゴは木から落ちるのか首をかしげた。
- Newton wondered why an apple falls down from the tree.
