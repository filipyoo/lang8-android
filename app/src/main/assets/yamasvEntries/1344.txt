
Using ところどころ

ところどころ means "here and there" or "some parts of something."

彼はところどころ穴の空いたシャツを着ている。
- He wears a shirt with some holes. 

道路のところどころに水たまりがあるから気をつけて。
- Be careful. There are puddles here and there on the street.

彼の作文にはところどころ文法の間違いがある。
- There are some grammatical mistakes in his essay.


あちこち also means "here and there," but ところどころ is used when the number is less than that in a case when you use あちこち. If you made 2 or 3 mistakes out of ten questions, I'd say ところどころ間違えている. If you made more than 5 mistakes, I'd say あちこち間違えている.
