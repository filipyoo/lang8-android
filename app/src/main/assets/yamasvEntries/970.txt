
Using お金を浮かせる

浮かせる/浮かす means to make something float.

この金属を水に浮かすことができると思いますか？
- Do you think it's possible to make this metal float on water?


お金を浮かせる means to scrimp by doing something instead of what you are supposed to do.

お金を浮かせるために新幹線じゃなくてバスで京都に行った。
- I went to Kyoto by bus instead of Shinkansen in order to save money. 

浮いたお金で美味しいものをたくさん食べた。
- I enjoyed a lot of delicious food with the money I saved.

自炊すれば月に2万円以上浮かせることができると思うよ。
- I think you can save 20,000 yen or more a month if you cook your own food.
