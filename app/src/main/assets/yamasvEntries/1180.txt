
Using どうせ

どうせ is used when you don't expect a good result.

一生懸命勉強してもどうせその試験には受からない。
- I wouldn't pass the test no matter how hard I studied.

最新式のPCを買っても、どうせ来年には2倍の速さのPCが出てくる。
- Even if you bought a cutting edge PC, they would sell another one that would be faster twice than it next year.

どうせ優勝できないにしても、一生懸命練習することが大切だ。
- It's important to practice hard even if you wouldn't become the champion.

どうせ当たらないのはわかっているけど、毎週宝くじを買っている。
- Although I understand the possibility to win the lottery is very low, I buy it every week.

彼女をデートに誘ったら。
どうせ断られる。
- Why don't you ask her out?
- She wouldn't say yes.
