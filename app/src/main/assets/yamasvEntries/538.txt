
The usage of 〜といい

You can use "〜といい" to describe your proposal or suggestion. It follows the dictionary form of volitional verbs in this case.

車が必要なら、私の車を使うといい。
- If you need a car, you might want to use my car.

日本の伝統文化に興味があるなら、京都に行くといい。
- If you are interested in Japanese traditional culture, you should visit Kyoto.

会話力を上げるために、スカイプでネイティブと話すといい。
- I recommend you to talk with a native speaker on Skype to improve your conversation skill.

自分の今の力を知るために、その試験を受けるといい。
- You should take the exam to recognize your current skills. 



When "〜といい" follows the potential form of verbs, it describes your hope.

日本旅行で京都にも行けるといい。
- I hope I can also visit Kyoto during my trip to Japan.

今度会った時は、長く話せるといいですね。
- I hope we can talk for a long time when we see next time.

彼のように早く走れるといいのに。
- I wish I could run faster like him.
