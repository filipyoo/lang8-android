
のお陰(かげ)で / のせいで

You can use 〜のお陰で when you thank someone or something.

私が今あるのは、あなたのお陰です - I owe what I am today to you.
あなたのお陰で、試験にパスしました - Thanks to you, I passed the exam.
薬のお陰で 頭痛がおさまった - Thanks to the medicine, my headache has gone.
ハイウェイのお陰で 早く着ける - Thanks to the highway, I can arrive at there in a short time.

You can also use 〜のお陰で when you blame someone or something.

あなたのお陰で いやな思いをした - I had a thin time thanks to you.
渋滞のお陰で 遅れた - I was late because of a traffic jam.


You can use 〜のせいで when you describe the reason of something.

彼は、働き過ぎのせいで 死んだ - He died from overwork.
渋滞のせいで 遅れた - I was late because of a traffic jam.


You can also use 〜のせいで when you blame someone or something. However, you can't use it when you thank someone or something.

○ あなたのせいで いやな思いをした - I had a thin time because of you.
○ 薬のせいで 吐き気がする - I feel nauseous because of the medicine.
