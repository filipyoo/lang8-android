
Using いちいち

いちいち means "every." It's mainly used when you are irritated.

寝る前に歯を磨きなさい。いちいち言わせないで。
- Brush your teeth before bed. Don't make me say it every night!

テレビのリモコンをなくしたから、チャンネルを変えるたびにいちいち立ち上がらないといけない。
- I lost TV remote, so I have to rise from my couch every time I change its channel.

彼のやることにいちいち腹が立つ。
- Every his action makes me irritated.

いちいち報告しなくていい。
- You don't have to report me everything.

いちいち全ての英単語を日本語に訳すと文が不自然になる。
- To translate every single English word into Japanese word makes your sentence unnatural.
