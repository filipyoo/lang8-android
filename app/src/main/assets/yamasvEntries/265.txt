
The Usage of つもり

つもり is used to mean "plan."

I'm going to go to a pool tomorrow.
明日はプールに行く つもり です。

つもり is also used to mean "mean."

そんなつもりで言ったんじゃない。
I didn't mean to say that.

「明日」って言ったつもりだった。
I meant to say "tomorrow."

そのつもりでやった / わざとやった。
I meant to do that.


It's okay to translate "what do you mean?" into "どういうつもり?" if you use it to blame someone. However, if you just want to ask someone's intention, you should say "どういう意味ですか?" 
"どういう意味ですか?" also sounds as if you are blaming someone, it depends on your tone of voice.
