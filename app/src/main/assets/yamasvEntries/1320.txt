
Using もう

もう昼ごはんは食べた。
- I've already had lunch.

もう昼ごはんは食べた？
- Have you had lunch yet?

彼はもう子供じゃない。
- He's not still a child.

These English sentences are a little confusing for Japanese people because you need to use different expressions to mean もう.

The opposite of もう is まだ.

まだ昼ごはんは食べてない。
- I haven't had lunch yet.

まだ昼ごはんを食べてないの？
- Haven't you had lunch yet?

彼はまだ子供だ。
- He's still a child.
