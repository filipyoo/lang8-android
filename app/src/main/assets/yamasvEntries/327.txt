
いっぱい With Time-related Expressions


When いっぱい is used with time-related expressions, it means "till the end of ....."

この仕事は午前中いっぱいかかります。
I'll do this job till noon. / This job will be finished at noon.

彼は、今週いっぱい休みです。
He's going to be off till the end of this week.

今月いっぱいこのホテルに泊まります。
I'm going to stay this hotel till the end of this month.

彼は、先月いっぱいで会社を辞めました。
He had left this company at the end of last month.

来年いっぱい景気はいいでしょう。
I think the economy will be growing till the end of next year.

今月いっぱいは、忙しくてジムには行けない。
I won't go to the gym for the rest of this month because I'm so busy.
