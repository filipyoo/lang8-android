
Using ましょうか？

"Do you want me to open the window?"
The literal translation of this sentence would be "窓を開けて欲しいですか?" However, it sounds slightly rude.  "窓を開けましょうか?" sounds nice. The casual expression would be "窓を開けようか?"

テレビを消しましょうか?
テレビを消そうか？
- Shall I turn the TV off?
(It's important to use a rising intnation. If you say this phrase with a flat intnation, it sounds as "Would you turn the TV off?"

どこで昼ごはんを食べましょうか?
どこで昼ごはんを食べようか?
- Where shall we have lunch?

今日はどの映画を見ましょうか？
今日はどの映画を見ようか？
- Which movie shall we watch today?
