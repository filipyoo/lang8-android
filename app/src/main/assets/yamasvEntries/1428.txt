
Expressions Related to 話

話を切り出す/話を持ち出す means "to bring up." 

話を切り出す has the nuance of "to change the subject suddenly." It's used when you had a plan to talk about something before a conversation starts. 持ち出す doesn't have the nuance.

離婚について友達に相談したかったけど、話を切り出せなかった。
- I wanted to ask advice from my friend about divorce, but I couldn't bring it up. 

挨拶の後、彼はいきなり合併について話を切り出した。
- After greeting, he suddenly brought up the merger matter.

彼女はいつも私の結婚式での恥ずかしい失敗の話を持ち出す。
- She always bring up my embarrassing blunder at my wedding ceremony.


話をそらす means to change the subject when you don't want to talk about something.

彼氏の話になると彼女はいつも話をそらしたがる。
- She always wants to change the subject when I ask her about her boyfriend.

話をそらさないで質問に答えなさい。
- Don't change the subject. Answer my question.


You can say 話を持っていく/持ってくる,

この話は社長のところに持って行きます。
- I'll discuss about the issue with the president.

彼が金への投資の話を持ってきた。
- He came and recommended investing in gold.
