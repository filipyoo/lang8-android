
〜し


You can list multiple reasons by adding し. から/ので is placed after the last reason in many cases.

美味しい し、新鮮だ し、安い から、あの店の料理が好きだ - I like that store's food, because those are delicious, fresh, and not expensive.
疲れている し、お金もない から、外出しない - I'll stay home because I'm tired and I'm broke.


You can use し after the last reason, and you can use し when you describe single reason. However, since these usages are rare, some Japanese people may say these are unnatural.

美味しい し、新鮮だ し、安い し、あの店の料理が好きだ.
疲れている し、外出しない.
