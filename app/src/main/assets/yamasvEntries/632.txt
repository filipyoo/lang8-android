
How to say "Who wants 〜?" in Japanese

For example, when you want to say "Who wants beer?" at a party,  you can say "ビールが欲しい人?" People who want beer would raise their hand and say "はい!" 


最後のピザが食べたい人？
- Who wants the last pizza?

ビリヤードがしたい人?
- Who wants to play billiard?

明日私と一緒に渋谷に行きたい人?
- Who wants to go to Shibuya with me tomorrow?


You don't have to say with a rising intonation. If you want to say them politely, you can say "〜人はいますか?" or "〜方はいらっしゃいますか?"
