
The Usage of 挑戦する

挑戦する means "to challenge," but this Japanese verb can take various nouns as the object. It is used when you try to do something you've never done.


今年は、日本語に挑戦したい。
- I'd like to start studying Japanese this year.

富士山に挑戦することに決めた。
- I've decided to try to climb to the highest point of Mount Fuji. 

寿司作りに挑戦しています。
- I'm learning how to make sushi.

日本旅行中、いろいろな日本料理に挑戦したい。
- I'd like to try various Japanese foods during my journey to Japan.

初めてフルマラソンに挑戦する。
- I'm going to try to run a full  marathon for the first time.
