
How to Say "No means no" in Japanese

You can say "ダメと言ったらダメ" to mean "No means no / When I say no, it means no."
You can use "〜 と言ったら 〜" when you express your strong will or opinions. It's often used when the speaker can't explain the reason logically. 

日本に行くと言ったら行く!
- I will go to Japan no matter what happens!

嫌（いや）と言ったら嫌！
- No way!


"彼が白と言ったら、黒も白 (If he says that it's white, it's white even if it's black)" is a common phrase which implies he's a dictator.
