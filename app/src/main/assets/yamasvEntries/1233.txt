
Using ぐっと

ぐっと is used when you put your strength.

彼は私の手をぐっと掴んだ。
- He held my hand tight.


It's also used when something much changes.

自動運転の車が普及したら、事故の数はぐっと減るだろう。
- If self-driving cars become popular, the number of car accidents would much decrease. 

二人ともアニメの大ファンだと知ってから、僕らはぐっと親しくなった。
- We became a lot closer after we realized that we both were big fans of anime.

このアプリを使うと、知らない言葉の意味を調べるのがぐっと楽になる。
- This app makes much easier to find out the meaning of words you don't know.
