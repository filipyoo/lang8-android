
Using かねる and かねない

The meaning of かねる and かねない might be counterintuitive. かねる means "can't" and かねない means "could." かねる sounds very formal and polite.

それは個人情報なのでお教えかねます。
- We're afraid, but we can't tell you them since those are personal information.

彼が何時に返ってくるかは分かりかねます。
- I don't know when he'll come. 

その申し出は受けかねます。
- We can't accept your offer.


かねない is mainly used when something bad could happen.

銀行が融資してくれなかったら我が社は倒産しかねない。
- Our company could be bankrupted if the bank refuses a loan.

赤ちゃんが間違って飲みかねないから、その薬は赤ちゃんの手の届かないところに置いたほうがいい。
- You must put the medicine in a place where your baby can't reach. She could eat it by mistake.

自動運転車を開発できない自動車メーカーはグーグルの下請けになりかねない。
- A car company that can't develop autonomous cars could become a subcontractor of Google.
