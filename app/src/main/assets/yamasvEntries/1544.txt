
Using 置いていく

置いていく means to leave something.

このバッグは大きすぎるからホテルに置いていく。
- This bag is too big, so I'll leave it in my hotel room.

You can use it for a person.

東京に転勤になるけど、家族は置いていく。
- I'm being transferred to Tokyo, but I'll leave my family here.


The past tense of it is 置いてきた.

鍵を机の上に置いてきた。
- I left my key on the desk. 
You can say like this both when you left it intentionally or unintentionally.

カメラを家に置いてきたことを後悔している。スマホじゃいい写真は撮れない。
- I'm regretting to leave my camera in my house. You can't take good pictures by a smartphone.
