
Using すぐ

すぐ/すぐに means "soon."

すぐに戻ります。
- I'll be back soon.

彼はすぐに有名な役者になるだろう。
- He'll become a famous actor soon.


It's also used to mean "(right ) now."

すぐに警察を呼びなさい。
- Call the police right now.

すぐに直します。
- I'll fix it now.


It's also used to mean "often" or "with ease."

僕の車はすぐ壊れる。日本車を買うべきだった。
- My car often breaks down. I should have bought a Japanese one.

このアプリを使って、知らない単語をすぐに調べられる。
- Using this app, you can look up word you don't know with ease.

この機械は複雑だからすぐには直せない。
- It's a complicated machine, so it's not easy to fix.


すぐ and すぐに are interchangeable in all the sentence but the last one; すぐは～ない sounds a little unnatural.
