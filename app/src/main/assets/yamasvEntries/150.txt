
〜につき

It has different usages.

First, it means "per/each."

入場料は、一人につき、1000円です。
The admission fee is one thousand yen per person.

駐車料金は、一時間につき、500円です。
The parking fee is five hundred yen an hour.

一回の買い物につき、スタンプが１つもらえます。
You can get a stamp with each purchase.


Next, you can also use it to describe reasons. It is mainly used in written language.

その試合は、悪天候につき、中止された。
The game has been canceled due to the bad weather.	

It is often used in public signs which warns or prohibits something.

自動ドアにつき、注意。

Finally, it is also used to say "about …." It is a formal expression of について.

この問題につき、議論します。(= その問題について、議論します。)
We will discuss the matter.
