
Using 〜のも頷ける(うなずける)

〜のも頷ける is used when you find out a good reason of something.

彼女はとてもわがままだった。彼がすごく怒ったのも頷ける。
- She was very selfish. He had good reason to get very angry.

彼はとてもいい選手だ。彼が高い給料をもらっているのも頷ける。
- He is a very good player. He has good reason to get a high salary.
 
この店の料理はとても美味しい。この店がいつも混んでいるのも頷ける。
- The dishes in this restaurant are delicious. No wonder this restaurant is always busy.

彼女はきれいなだけでなく頭もいい。すごくもてるのも頷ける。
- Not only she is beautiful, but also smart. No wonder she is very popular with the guys.


(According to my dictionary, both うなずける and うなづける are correct.)
