
Using 〜くらいで

〜くらいで is used when you do something because of something trifling.

チョコレートを食べられたくらいで怒るな。
- Don't be upset just because someone ate your chocolate.

ほとんどの日本人は地震で少しビルが揺れたくらいでは動揺しない。
- Most Japanese people don't panic just because a building shakes a little in an earthquake.

ひらがなを全部読めたくらいで日本語をマスターしたとは言えない。
- You can't say that you've mastered Japanese just because you can read all hiragana.

一回勝ったくらいで満足してはいけない。彼らより強いチームはたくさんいる。
- You shouldn't be satisfied just because you won once. There are a lot of teams stronger than them.
