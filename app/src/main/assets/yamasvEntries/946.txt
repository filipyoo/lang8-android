
Using 構わない

〜ても構わない means "don't care/mind."

裏切り者と呼ばれても構わない。
- I don't care if they call me a betrayer.

好きなだけここにいても構わない。
- You can stay here as long as you want.

タバコを吸っても構わないですか？
構わないですよ。
- Do you mind if I smoke?
- No, I don't.


構うもんか is a casual version of 構わない。

そのピンクのスーツ着てると馬鹿に見えるよ。
構うもんか。
- That pink suit makes you look stupid.
- I don't care.


(私に)構わないで means "Don't worry about me" or "Leave me alone."


お構いなく is a common phrase to mean "Don't trouble yourself." It's used when you visit someone's house and they try to serve you a lot.
