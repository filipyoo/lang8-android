
The Dictionary Form of Verbs + には + an Adjective

When には follows the dictionary form of verbs and is followed by an adjective, it means "it's too... To..."

家まで歩くには遠い。
- It's too far to walk to my house.

寝るには早い。
- It's too early to go to bed.

誰かに電話するには遅い。
- It's too late to call someone.

プールで泳ぐには寒い。
- It's too cold to swim in the pool.
