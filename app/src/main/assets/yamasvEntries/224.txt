
The Usage of 方がいい.

1.
When it is used to recommend someone to do something, 方がいい follows タ-form of verbs. 

早く寝た方がいい。
You should go to bed soon.

車で行った方がいい。
You should go by car.


You should also use た方がいい when you ask someone's desire.

窓を閉めた方がいいですか？
Do you want me to close the window?


2.
When it is used to describe your desire after comparison, 方がいい follows the dictionary form of verbs.

外出するより、家でゲームをする方がいい。
I prefer playing video game at home than going out.

肉より、野菜を食べる方がいい。
I would like to eat vegetables instead of meats.



In case of recommending someone to do something after comparison, you should use た方がいい。

肉より、野菜を食べた方がいい。
You should eat vegetables instead of meats.
