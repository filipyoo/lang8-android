
Using あっち/むこう 

When you compare you and someone else (not the listener,) you can call them あっち/むこう and yourself こっち.

彼と僕は双子だけど、あっち/むこう のほうが女の子にもてる。
- He and I are twins, but he's more popular with girls.

スポーツは あっち/むこう のほうが上手いけど、数学はこっちのほうが得意だ。
- He's better at sports than I am, but I'm better at math than he is.


When you compare you and the listener, you can call the listener そっち. It's mainly used you are arguing with someone.

そっちが先に殴ったんだろ！
- You hit me first!

そっちが訴えるなら、こっちも訴える。
- If you sue me, I'll sue you.
