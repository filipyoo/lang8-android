
Expressions Related to Face


強面(こわもて) means "a scary face" like this: <a href="http://goo.gl/symVaS" target="_blank">http://goo.gl/symVaS</a>
(He often appears in movies as a bad guy, but the actor himself is a nice and kind person.)

あの強面の男はヤクザですか？
- Is that guy with a scary face a Yakuza?


童顔(どうがん) means "a baby face."

私は童顔なので、タバコを買おうとする時にいつも歳を聞かれます。
- Since I have a baby face, they always ask my age when I try to buy a cigarette.


しかめっ面(つら) means "a frown face."

私達のボスはいつもしかめっ面をしているから、不愉快だ。
- Our boss is always frowning, that makes us unhappy.


〜面(づら) is sometimes used when you pretend to be some kind of person.

被害者面しないで！
- Don't play victim. 

彼は善人面しているけど、実は悪いやつだ。
- Although he pretends to be a good person, he is actually wicked.
