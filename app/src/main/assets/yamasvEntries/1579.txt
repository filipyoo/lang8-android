
Using 凹む

凹む(へこむ) means to cave in or to be dented. I think you can easily memorize this kanji because it clearly represents its meaning.

壁を蹴ったら凹んだ。
- The wall caved in when I kicked it.

この缶は簡単に凹む。
- This can yields easily to pressure.

凹んだ方を上にしてスプーンをテーブルに置いてください。
- Put the spoon concave side up on the table.

It's also used as a slang word that means to feel down.

テストに落ちて凹んでいます。
- I failed the test and I'm feeling down.

彼女に振られて彼はかなり凹んでいる。
- Dumped by her, he's really depressed.

そのニュースを聞いたら彼は凹むだろう。
- He'll be bummed if he hears the news.
