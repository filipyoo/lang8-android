
Using 切れる

切れる means "sharp" or "cut wel" when it's used for bladed objects.

このナイフは切れる
- This knife cuts well. / This knife is sharp.


It can also be used to mean "smart" when it's used for a person.

彼は切れる。
- He is smart.

It also be used to mean "to go postal" or "to flip out." In this case, katakana is mainly used.

彼は思い通りに行かないといつもキレる。
- He flips out whenever something doesn't go as planned.



You can also use 切れる to mean "to go out" or "to run out."

蛍光灯が切れそうだ。
- The fluorescent lamp is going out.

IPhoneのバッテリーがまた切れた。
- The battery in my iPhone have died again.

ガソリンが切れたので、スタンドまで押さないといけなかった。
- My car had run out of gas, so we had to push it to the station.
(スタンド means "gas station.")

すいません。在庫を切らしてます。
- I'm sorry. We are out of stock.
