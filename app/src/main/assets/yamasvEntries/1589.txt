
Using とびきり

とびきり is an adverb that means "extraordinary", "extremely" or something like this.

東京で食べた鮨はとびきりおいしかった。
- Sushi we had in Tokyo was extraordinary delicious.

この季節の夕焼けはとびきりきれいだ。
- The sunset in this season is extremely beautiful.

彼女はとびきり偉大なアーティストだ。
- She is as great an artist as ever lived.

その大学に入るのはとびきり難しい。
- It's unbelievably difficult to enter the university.


とびきりの can modify a noun.

彼女はいつもとびきりの笑顔を見せてくれる。
- She always shows me the sweetest smile.

彼はとびきりの美人と結婚した。
- He married a strikingly beautiful girl.
