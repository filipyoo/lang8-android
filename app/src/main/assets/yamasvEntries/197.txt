
The usage of まず for describing your confidence.

You can use まず to describe your confidence for your judges or guesses.

彼が犯人なのは、まず まちがいない。
- I know he is the criminal.
- It's no doubt that he is the criminal.

その問題はむずかしすぎるので、まず ほとんどの学生には解けないだろう。
- I think that most students cannot solve the problem because it is too hard.

今出かけたとしても、まず 間に合わないだろう。
- It's no doubt that we won't make it in time even if we leave now.

かなり練習しないと、あのチームには、まず 負ける。
- We couldn't beat that team unless we practice very hard.
