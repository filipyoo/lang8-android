
The Usage of 何を思ったのか

何を思ったのか is used when someone suddenly starts something and you don't understand why.


何を思ったのか、彼は急に会社を辞めて、料理の勉強を始めた。
- I don't know why, but he suddenly quitted his job and started learning how to cook.

何を思ったのか、彼は日本に行くと言い出した。
- I don't understand why, but he suddenly says that he's going to Japan.


It's sometimes used when you talk about yourself.

何を思ったのか、40過ぎてからバイクの免許を取る気になった。
- I don't know why, but I've decided to take a license of the motorcycle even though I'm over 40.
