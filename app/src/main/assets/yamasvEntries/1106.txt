
How to Say "to send someone" in Japanese

送る is used only when you send things.
You can say 来させる to mean "to send someone" to where you are.

手伝いが要る。誰か来させてくれる？
- I need someone to help me. Can you send someone for me?

You can say 行かせる to mean "to send someone" to where the listener is.

息子を手伝いに行かせる。
- I'll send my son to help you.


誰がここに来させた? / 誰に言われて来た?
- Who sent you here?

誰をそのミーティングに行かせようか？
- Who should I send to the meeting?
