
Using "Verb + なんて"

When you have a strong impression for some actions, you can say なんて after the verb. 

そういうふうに考えるなんて変だ。
- It's strange that you should think like that.

私に嘘をつくなんて驚いた。
- I'm surprised that you should lie to me.

彼の言ったことをみんな信じるなんておかしい。
- It's funny that everybody should believe what he said.

彼が医者になりたいなんて信じられない。
- It's unbelievable that he should want to be a doctor.
