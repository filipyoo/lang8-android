
The Usage of "〜ことだ"

"〜ことだ" is used when you describe a definition of something.

「借金」というのは、お金を人に借りることだ。
- "Debt" means "to borrow money."

「移民」というのは、別な国から永住するために来た人のことです。
- "Immigrant " is a person who has come to live permanently in a country that is not their own.

It's also used to describe the implicit meaning. In this case, "ということだ" is often used.

お金を人に借りるということは、弱みを握られるということだ。
- To borrow money from someone means to let them hold your weak point.

外国語を学ぶということは、同時にその国の文化を学ぶということだ。
- To study foreign language also means to study the culture of the country.



"〜ことだ" is also used when you describe what someone should do to accomplish their purpose.

外国語をマスターしたいなら、ネイティブと友だちになることだ。
- You should make a friend with a native speaker if you want to master a foreign language.

日本に行きたいなら、節約することだ。
- You should save money if you want to visit Japan.

長生きしたいなら、タバコはやめることだ。
- You should stop smoking if you want to live longer.



"〜ことだ" is also used to say hearsay.

彼は会社を辞めるということだ。
- I heard that he's going to leave this company.

その事故で３人がケガしたということだ。
- They say that three people were injured by the accident.
