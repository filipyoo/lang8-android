
Using 出てこない

You can say 出てこない when something slipped your mind. It sounds more natural than 思い出せない in a casual conversation.

彼の名前が出てこない。
- His name slipped my mind.

この漢字の読み方は知ってたけど、テストの時は出てこなかった。
- I knew how to read this kanji, but it slipped my mind when I took the test.

子供の時夢中だったアニメのタイトルが出てこない。
- I can't remember the title of the anime what I was really into in my childhood.

日本語の読み書きはできるけど、日本人と話す時は日本語がうまく出てこない。
- I can read/write Japanese, but it won't come out of my mouth smoothly when I talk with a native speaker.
