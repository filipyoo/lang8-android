
Using 飽きる

飽きる means "to get tired of doing something" or "to get bored." 

ラーメンを食べるのは飽きた。もう５日連続で食べている。
- I get tired of having ramen. I have ramen five days in a row.

ラーメンは飽きた sounds more natural when it's obvious that you are talking about having it, not making or seeing it.

飽きる can follow the masu stem of a verb, so you can also say ラーメンは食べ飽きた.

この歌は聞き飽きた。ラジオをつけるといつもこの歌が流れてくる。
- I got tired of hearing that song. It's playing every time I turn on the radio.

この景色は見飽きることがない。
- You never get tired of seeing this view.

このCMは見飽きた。新しいのに替えるべきだ。
- I've watched enough of this commercial film. They should release the new one.
