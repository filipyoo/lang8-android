
〜うものなら

"[volitional form of verb] + ものなら" means "if." It is used when some actions will bring serious results.

あのボスの言うことに逆らお うものなら、すぐに仕事を失うだろう。
If you go against what the boss says, you will lose your job soon.

そんな提案をしよ うものなら、彼は相当起こるだろう。
If we make a proposal like that, he will be so angry.

今、彼女に何か言お うものなら, 彼女は泣き出すだろう。
If you say anything to her now, she will start to cry.

この崖から落ちよ うものなら、まちがいなく死ぬ。
If you fell over this precipice, you would definitely die.


The following sentences sound very strange because it is not a serious situation.

明日雨が降ろ うものなら、コーヒーを飲む。
If it rains tomorrow, I will drink coffee.

学校に行こ うものなら、友だちと話せる。
If I go to school, I will be able to talk with my friends.
