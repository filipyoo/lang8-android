
Using 押さえる

押さえる(おさえる) means to hold/press down.

はしごをしっかり押さえておいてくれる？
- Can you hold the ladder firmly?


It's also used to mean to keep something.

ミーティングルームを押さえておきます。
- I'll keep a meeting room.

4月に東京に行くなら早めにホテルを押さえておいたほうがいい。
- You should make a reservation for a hotel early if you are going to Tokyo in April.


It's also used to mean to understand/master something important.

何をするにも基本を押さえることがとても大切です。
- It's very important to master the basics no matter what you do.

健康がいちばん大切だということを押さえておくべき。
- You have to bear in mind that nothing is more important than health.
