
Using 次第

When 次第(しだい) follows a noun, it means "to depend on" or "up to."

この文の意味は文脈次第です。
- The meaning of the sentence depends on the context.

そのオファーを受けるかどうかは報酬次第です。
- Whether I'll accept the offer or not depends on the fee.

そのプロジェクトの成功は君の努力次第だ。
- The success of the project is up to how hard you work.


次第 can also follow the masu stem of a verb to mean "as soon as."

彼が着き次第教えます。
- I'll let you know as soon as he arrives.

次の給料をもらい次第仕事を辞める。
- I'll quit my job as soon as I receive next pay.

お金が貯まり次第日本に行きたい。
- I'd like to visit Japan soon after I save enough money.
