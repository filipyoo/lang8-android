
Using 極まる

極まる(きわまる) means "to exceed the limit." It's usually used to mean "extremely." It follows the stem of a na-adjective.

この映画は退屈極まる。
- This movie is extremely boring.

あのレストランのウエイターの態度は失礼極まる。
- The attitudes of the waiters of that restaurant is extremely rude.

彼は贅沢極まる家に住んでいる。
- He lives in an extremely luxury house.


Interestingly, 極まりない, not 極まらない, also means "extremely," so 失礼極まる and 失礼極まりない have the same meaning.
