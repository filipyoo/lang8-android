
Using 前後

前後(ぜんご) means "the front and the rear" or "forward and behind."

車を動かす前に前後をよく確認しなさい。
- Look carefully forward and behind your car before you move it.

車の前後にナンバープレートをつけないといけません。
- You have to display license plates at the front and the rear of your car.


It can also follow a number to mean "around." It sounds slightly more formal than くらい.

駅までタクシーで50分前後かかる。
- It'll take around fifty minutes to the station by taxi.

新聞によると2000人前後の人がそのデモに参加した。
- The newspaper says that around 2,000 people joined the protest march.

その車を修理するのに5,000ドル前後かかると思う。
- I think It'll take around 5,000 dollars to fix the car.

東京では今年は3月20日前後に桜が咲き始める。
- In Tokyo, the cherry blossoms will start blooming on around 20th March this year.
