
Using かもね

かもね is used to describe your guess in a casual conversation. It follows a phrase.

彼は今シーズンすごく調子がいいから、次のレースでは世界記録を出すかもね。
- He keeps very good condition this season; he could set a new world record in the next race.

台風が近づいているから、今日は家にいたほうがいいかもね。
- A typhoon is coming. Maybe, we should stay home today.

イチローがもっと早くからメジャーリーグでプレーしていたら、5000本ヒットを打ったかもね。
- If Ichiro started his carrier in the MLB earlier, he might have reached 5,000 hits.


You can use it by itself.

彼女はモデル？すごくきれい。
かもね。
- Is she a model? She's so pretty.
- Could be.


Some men might say かもな instead, but it could sound slightly rude.
