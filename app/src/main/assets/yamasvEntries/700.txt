
Using いつも

いつも is mainly used to mean "always."

私はいつも昼食の後にコーヒーを飲みます。
- I always drink coffee after lunch.


いつも is sometimes used as an exaggerated expression.

彼はいつもゲームをしている。
- He always plays video games.
(Of course, this sentence doesn't mean that he doesn't sleep or eat at all.)


いつも is also used to mean "usually."

いつも仕事の後は何をしてるの？
The speaker doesn't think that the listener always does the same thing after work. They just want to ask "What do you USUALLY do after work?" You can answer like this:
いつもはゲームをしたりテレビを見たりしてます。
- I usually play video games or watch TV.
(いつもは sounds slightly natural than いつも in this sentence. "いつも〜します" sounds "I always 〜." "いつもは〜します" sounds "I usually 〜.")

I always go to bed at eleven. いつも(必ず)、 11時に寝る。
I usually go to bed at eleven. いつもは、11時に寝る。
