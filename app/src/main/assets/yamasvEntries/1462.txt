
Using 残り～

You can say "残り(のこり)～" to mean "～ to go."

(レースは)残り3周。
- Three laps to go.

テストまで残り2周間しかない。
- There are only two weeks to go until the exam.

金曜日までにこの本を読み終えてレポートを書かないといけないけどまだ残り5章もある。
- I have to finish reading this book and write a report by Friday, but there are still five chapters to go.

You can also say 後(あと) instead of 残り.
