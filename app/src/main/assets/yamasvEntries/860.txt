
Using 落とす

落とす means to drop.

うっかり高い茶碗を落としたけど、運良く割れなかった。
- I dropped an expensive cup carelessly, but fortunately, it wasn't  broken.


When the object is a small stuff you usually put into your pocket, such as a wallet or key, it's also used to mean to lose.

旅行中に財布を落とした。
- I lost my wallet during the journey.

鍵を落とさないように気をつけて。
- Be careful not to lose the key.

It also can be used for a game/match.

ヤンキースは大事な試合を落とした。今季は優勝はできないだろう。
- The Yankees lost the important match. They wouldn't be able to win the championship this season.


お金を落とす means not only to lose money, but also "to spend money in some area."

たくさんの旅行者がこの街を訪れて、お金を落としてくれることを望む。
- We hope that a lot of travelers will visit our town and spend much money.


命を落とす means to die.

その事故でたくさんの人が命を落とした。
- A lot of people died from the accident.


気を落とす means to be discouraged.

彼女はその結果に気を落としている。
- She is discouraged about the result.
