
The Meaning of "見合わせる"

"顔を見合わせる" means "to look at each other."

我々はすごく驚いて、互いに顔を見合わせた。
- We were very surprised and looked at each other.


"見合わせる" also means "to postpone / suspend / withhold."

父親が病気なので、旅行は見合わせることにした。
- I've decided to put off my journey because my father is sick.

世界的な不景気はしばらく続くから、株を買うのは見合わせた方がいい。
- You should postpone buying stocks because the worldwide recession will continue for a while.

今回はそのフォーラムに参加するのは見合わせたいと思います。
- I have to put off the opportunity to attend the forum this time.

その車の新しいモデルがすぐに出るらしいので、買うのは見合わせた。
- I postponed purchase of the car because they say that the new model of the car will be released soon.
