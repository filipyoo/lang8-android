
Using やる

やる can be used when you accomplish something awesome.

やった！　テストに受かった!
- Yes! I made it! I passed the test.

やったね。
- You made it!

彼はやるね。今月もう10台車を売ったよ。
- He's pretty shrewd. He has already sold ten cars this month.


You can also use it as the pro-verb of a suru-verb instead of する.

宿題はいつするの？
後でやる。
- When will you do your homework?
- I'll do it later.

毎朝ジョギングをやるようになってから10キロやせた。
- I lost ten kilos after I started jogging every morning.

彼はすぐやると言ったけど、まだやってない。
- He said he'd do it right now, but he hasn't done it yet.
