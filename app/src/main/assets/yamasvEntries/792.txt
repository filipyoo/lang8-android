
Using 腐る

腐る is used when a food goes bad.

その牛乳は冷蔵庫にしまいなさい。そうしないとすぐ腐るよ。
Keep the milk in the refrigerator, or it'll go bad soon.


It's also used to describe person who are sulking.

彼はちゃんとした理由もなしに解雇されたので腐っている。
He is sulking because he was fired without good reason.

小さいことで腐るのは止めなよ。
Stop sulking over small things.


"腐ったみかん" is used as a metaphor for a person who brings bad effects on other people. It's because if you put one rotten orange into a box filled with a lot of fresh ones, the other ones go bad soon.
