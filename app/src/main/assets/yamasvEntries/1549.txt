
Using 悩む

悩む(なやむ) is used when you don't know what should you do. It's used both in serious situations and not serious situations.

バニラアイスを買うかチョコアイスを買うかで悩む。
- I'm wondering if I should buy a vanilla ice cream or chocolate one.

妻と離婚すべきかどうか悩む。
- I don't know if I should divorce my wife.

どの学校に行くかまだ悩んでいる。
- I haven't decided which school I will go yet.


It's also used to mean to be worried about something.

彼は息子の病気のことで悩んでいる。
- He's worries\d about his son's sick.

大学を卒業してからの人生について悩んでいる。
- I'm worried about my future after graduating university.


悩んでいる is also used when you are suffered from something. The particle に is used after the object in this case.

その工場の近くに住む人はみな、工場からの騒音に悩んでいる。
- Every person who lives around the factory is suffering from the loud noise from it.

私は頭痛に悩んでいる。
- I'm suffering from headaches.
