
Using 〜まで to Mean "before"

〜まで is used to show a date or time before something will happen. It can follow a clause or noun.

試合が始まるまで２時間ある。(=試合まで２時間ある)
It's two hours before the game starts.

夏休みまで３週間です。
It's three weeks before my summer vacation.

飛行機が離陸するまで10分しかなかった。
I had only ten minutes before my plane took off.

Be careful. You can't say 私の飛行機 unless you own the plane. If you want to say 私の to clarify which plane you are supposed to get on, you need to say 私の乗る飛行機.

あれが私の飛行機です。
That's my private plane.

あれが私の乗る飛行機です。
That's the plane that I am supposed to get on.
