
Using ～もすれば

When もすれば follows a time, it means "within...."

30年もすれば、誰でも宇宙に行けるようになる。
- Anyone would be able to go to space within 30 years.

足を骨折したけど、医者は2,3週間もすれば歩けるようになると言ってた。
- I broke my leg, but the doctor said that I would be able to walk within a few weeks.

日本語の挨拶を幾つか覚えたけど、使わなかったら一週間もすれば全部忘れるだろう。
- I've learned some Japanese greetings, but I'll forget all of them within one week if I don't use them at all.


もすれば implies "at latest." If you say すれば instead, it doesn't.

あなたのパスポートは3ヶ月すれば切れます。
- Your passport will expire in 3 months.
