
〜とあって

〜とあって means "because 〜." とあって comes after a noun phrase or sentence which describes special appearance or situation. It is often used to describe the speaker's observations.


土曜の夜 とあって、そのレストランはとても混んでいた。
Since it was a Saturday night, the restaurant was very crowded.

雪を見るのは初めて とあって、彼女はすごくうれしそうだった。
Since it was her first time seeing snow, she looked very happy.

２回も試験に落ちた とあって、彼は落ち込んでいる。
Since he had failed the exam twice, he is feeling discouraged.
